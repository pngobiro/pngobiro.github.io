B. The Nilotes

  • - The Nilotes migrated from Southern Sudan.
  • - The Nilotes are divided into three'
    1. Highland Nilotes
    2. Plain Nilotes
    3. River-lake Nilotes


Nilotes found in Eastern Africa are;

a) Kenya

Highland Nilotes:

  • Kipsigis, Nandi, Tugen, Keiyo, Pokot, Bok, Bongomek, Sabaot, Terik, Ogiek, Marakwet

River lake Nilotes:

  • Luo

Plain Nilotes:

  • Samburu, Maasai, Iteso, Njemps, Turkana

b) Uganda

Highland Nilotes:

- Sebei

River lake Nilotes:

- Alur, Lugbara, Lango, Jopadhola, Acholi

Plain Nilotes:

- Karamajong, Jie, Iteso

c) Tanzania

Highland Nilotes:

- Dadoga

Plain Nilotes:

- Maasai

d) South Sudan

River lake Nilotes:

- Dinka, Nuer, Anuak, Ajur, Chilluk
