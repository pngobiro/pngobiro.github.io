Dental Technology 1

Duration: 16 hours Level: Introductory

Course Overview

This course provides an introduction to dental technology, covering fundamental aspects of dental diseases, hygiene practices, dental equipment, and their maintenance. Students will learn about the basic principles of dentistry, common dental diseases, preventive hygiene measures, and the operation and maintenance of essential dental equipment used in dental procedures.

Course Modules

  1. Dental Diseases and Hygiene

    Introduction to dental diseases, dental caries, periodontal disease, and dental hygiene practices.

    60 minutes

  2. Dental Anatomy

    Overview of dentistry, tooth structure, types of teeth, and dental formulas.

    60 minutes

  3. Development of Teeth

    Stages of tooth development, deciduous and permanent dentition.

    45 minutes

  4. Dental Equipment - Introduction

    Basic dental equipment, teeth treating procedures, and necessary supplies.

    75 minutes

  5. Dental Equipment - Safety and Maintenance (Part 1)

    Safety aspects, oil separator cleaning, compressor air lines, and function tests.

    60 minutes

  6. Air Turbine and Syringe Systems

    Air turbine operation, arrangement, and triple syringe & cautery units.

    75 minutes

  7. Dental Unit Layout and Tooth Numbering

    Typical dental department layout and tooth numbering systems.

    60 minutes

  8. Dental Equipment - Supplies and Turbine Handpiece

    Detailed look at dental unit supplies and the turbine handpiece.

    75 minutes

  9. Handpiece Mechanisms

    Air cushion and ball bearing mechanisms in handpieces, micro-motor handpiece.

    75 minutes

  10. Sprayvit, Aspirator and Auxiliary Equipment

    Sprayvit, aspirator, suction nozzle, tumbler filling, cuspidor bowl, and dental light.

    75 minutes

  11. Dental Unit Accessories

    Ultrasonic handpiece, fibre optic light, and surgery handpiece.

    60 minutes

  12. Hydraulic and Pneumatic Systems

    Medium flow in hydraulic/pneumatic elements and maintenance.

    75 minutes

  13. Dental Equipment - Maintenance (Part 2)

    Monthly and every 3 months maintenance schedules and safety aspects.

    60 minutes

  14. Dental Unit Supplies - Detailed Requirements

    In-depth review of dental unit supplies and their requirements including water and compressed air.

    75 minutes

  15. Dental Chairs - Hydraulic and Electro-hydraulic

    Detailed explanation of hydraulic and electro-hydraulic dental chairs.

    75 minutes

  16. Dental Chairs - Electro-mechanical and Maintenance

    Electro-mechanical dental chairs and maintenance checklists.

    75 minutes

  17. Pneumatic and Hydraulic Diagrams

    Exploration of pneumatic and hydraulic diagrams for dental units.

    60 minutes

Prerequisites

  • No prior knowledge of dental technology is required. Basic understanding of medical terminology is helpful.
