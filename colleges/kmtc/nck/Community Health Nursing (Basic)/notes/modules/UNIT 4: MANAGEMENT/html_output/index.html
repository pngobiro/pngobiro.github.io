UNIT 4: MANAGEMENT

An introduction to management concepts, principles, functions, and their application in nursing care services.

Table of Contents

  • Section 1: The Concept of Management

    An introduction to the meaning of management, its definitions, and the historical development of management theories including Classical, Human Relations, Systems, and Contingency theories.
  • Section 2: Leadership and Principles of Management

    Covers leadership styles (Authoritarian, Democratic, Laissez-faire), leadership theories, and core management principles such as Division of Labour, Unity of Command, and Discipline.
  • Section 3: Functions of Management

    Details the five main functions of management: Planning, Staffing, Organising, Delegating, and Controlling, forming the basis of the manager's day-to-day work.
  • Section 4: Managing Resources, Change, and Health Information Systems

    Explores the management of human, financial, material, and time resources. Also discusses managing organizational change and the role of Health Information Systems (HIS) in modern healthcare.
  • Section 5: Managing Nursing Care Services

    Applies management concepts to nursing, covering different types of healthcare organizations, nursing care delivery systems (Case, Functional, Team, Primary), and related legal and ethical issues.
