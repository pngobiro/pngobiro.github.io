# NCK Examination Instructions and Schedule (Feb & May 2026)

## General Instructions
*   **Duration:** Each paper is allotted **2 hours**.
*   **Sessions:**
    *   Morning: Starts at **9:00 a.m.**
    *   Afternoon: Starts at **2:00 p.m.**
*   **Rehearsal:** Attendance is **MANDATORY** in person. Must complete mock exams before main exam.
*   **Arrival:** Arrive at assigned seat **1 hour before** scheduled start time. Punctuality is essential (no entry after 30 mins from start).
*   **Requirements:** National ID and NCK Exam Card are mandatory for admission.
*   **Dress Code:** Professional dress code or official uniform (for Kenya-trained candidates). No casual/revealing attire.
*   **Prohibited Items:** Mobile phones, smartwatches, earbuds, books, notes. Only the PC for the exam is allowed.
*   **System:** Online examination application only. No other apps/browsers allowed in background.
