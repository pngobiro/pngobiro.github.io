<strong>Apply Soap</strong>
Take hands out of the water to add quarter-waist portion soap.</p>

<strong>Rub Palms Together</strong>

<p><img src="assets/images/image-20260125-5e21592b.jpeg" alt="img-3.jpeg">
<strong>Rub Backs of Hands</strong>
Use your right palm to massage the back of your left hand. Switch hands after 2 seconds.</p>
<p><img src="assets/images/image-20260125-5d9879c9.jpeg" alt="img-4.jpeg">
<strong>Interlace Fingers</strong>
Rub palms together. With fingers (illiterace), clean the underside of your fingers by rubbing them along your knuckles.</p>
<p><img src="assets/images/image-20260125-f2e3bf88.jpeg" alt="img-5.jpeg">
<strong>Scrub Thumbs</strong>
Clasp right hand over the left thumb and rotate for 3 seconds. Repeat on the right thumb.</p>
<p><img src="assets/images/image-20260125-50dde77c.jpeg" alt="img-6.jpeg">
<strong>Rub Palms With Fingertips</strong>
Rotate your right hand fingers clockwise and then counterclockwise on the left palm. Switch hands after 2 seconds.</p>
<p><img src="assets/images/image-20260125-c4102c5e.jpeg" alt="img-7.jpeg">
<strong>Rinse</strong>
Wash soap away until the water runs clear and you no longer see suits. Turn the faucet off with elbow or paper towel.</p>
<p><img src="assets/images/image-20260125-cc0d8d08.jpeg" alt="img-8.jpeg">
<strong>Dry</strong>
Use a paper towel or hand dryer to dry your hands — not your scrubs!</p>

<div data-testid="stHeadingWithActionElements" class="st-emotion-cache-1p9ibxm e1icttdg2"><h1 level="1" id="measuring-vital-signs">MEASURING VITAL SIGNS<span data-testid="stHeaderActionElements" class="st-emotion-cache-gi0tri e1icttdg3"><a href="#measuring-vital-signs" class="st-emotion-cache-yinll1 e1icttdg1"><svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" width="16" height="16" viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round"><path d="M15 7h3a5 5 0 0 1 5 5 5 5 0 0 1-5 5h-3m-6 0H6a5 5 0 0 1-5-5 5 5 0 0 1 5-5h3"></path><line x1="8" y1="12" x2="16" y2="12"></line></svg></a></span></h1></div>
<p>It is the process of measuring temperature, pulse, respiration, blood pressure and pain of the client /patient in order to detect any deviations</p>
<p>Temperature assessment –involves measuring the balance between heat production and heat loss by the body</p>
<p>Pulse assessment –is the measurement of pressure pulsation created when heart contracts and ejects blood in to the aorta creating a wave of expansion and recoil in the arteries which can be felt by palpation on a point where an artery crosses a bone close to the surface</p>
<p>Pulse oximetry –is the measurement of arterial oxygen saturation using non-invasive light</p>
<p>Respirations assessment –is the measurement of the breathing pattern and rate</p>
<p>Pain assessment- is evaluation of reported pain and the factors that alleviate or exacerbate it, as well as response to treatment of pain</p>
