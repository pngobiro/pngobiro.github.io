Health Data Classification

Duration: 8 hours Level: Intermediate

Course Overview

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to the principles and practices of health data classification. We will explore the International Classification of Diseases (ICD) and the International Classification of Procedures in Medicine (ICPM), equipping you with the skills to accurately classify and code diseases, health conditions, and medical procedures. Through a structured learning path, you'll learn how these classification systems are used to standardize health data, facilitate research, and improve health service management.

    Explore the evolution and underlying principles of ICD classification.

    60 minutes

    Understand the structure of ICD including its chapters and categories.

    60 minutes

  4. Module 4: Purpose and Use of Disease Classification

    Examine the purpose and uses of disease classification.

    60 minutes

  5. Module 5: The 22 Board Categories and Organization of ICD and ICPM

    Overview of the 22 categories of diseases and the layout of ICD/ICPM
