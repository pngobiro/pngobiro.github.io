Data Quality Audit

Duration: 5 hours Level: Intermediate

Course Overview

This course provides a thorough understanding of data quality audits (DQAs) within health information systems. You will learn about the key principles and processes involved in conducting a DQA, the dimensions of data quality, and how to apply audit results for improvements in data management and program implementation. This course emphasizes the importance of reliable data in healthcare, enabling participants to contribute effectively to improving health outcomes through better data practices.

Course Modules

  1. Module 1: Introduction to Data Quality Audit (DQA)

    Learn about the importance and basic concepts of DQA and how data quality is measured.

    60 minutes

  2. Module 2: Improving Data Quality and Dimensions of Data Quality

    Explore strategies for improving data quality and understand the importance of quality dimensions.

    60 minutes

  3. Module 3: Detailed Data Quality Dimensions

    Explore in detail, how to ensure reliability, precision, timeliness, integrity and confidentiality.

    60 minutes

  4. Module 4: Principles of DQA

    Understand the underlying principles that guide the DQA process.

    60 minutes

  5. Module 5: DQA Processes

    Understand how to design and implement a DQA, including ethical considerations and post-audit procedures.

    60 minutes
