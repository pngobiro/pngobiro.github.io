Task:

Provide the candidate with the control and power circuit of a three-phase star-delta starter.

  3. Wire the control and power circuit using the circuit diagrams provided.

Safety Note: Ensure the main power is OFF before starting wiring. The Star-Delta starter reduces starting current by initially connecting windings in Star (voltage per phase = $V_L/\sqrt{3}$), then switching to Delta (voltage per phase = $V_L$) after a set time delay. The electrical interlocks (NC contacts of K2 in K3 circuit and vice versa) are critical to prevent a short circuit between phases during transition.
