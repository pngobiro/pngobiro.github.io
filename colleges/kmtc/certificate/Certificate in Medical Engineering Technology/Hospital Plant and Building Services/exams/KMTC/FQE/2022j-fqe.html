1.
Which one of the following is not true about use of copper pipe in gas installation?
a) Copper pipes can be joined quickly
b) Copper pipes have smooth internal bore
c) Copper pipes are flexible
d) Hemp sealing can be used I copper pipe gas joints
Show Answer

Correct Answer: d) Hemp sealing can be used I copper pipe gas joints

Explanation: Hemp and paste are typically used for threaded iron pipes. Copper pipes are joined using compression fittings (with olives) or capillary solder fittings. Hemp is not suitable for copper compression or capillary joints.

b) Urinal
c) Wash hand basin

Explanation: Water Closets and Urinals discharge "soil water" (human excreta). Wash hand basins discharge "waste water" (grey water).

3.

a) Non generated
b) Aerated

d) Gas water heaters

Correct Answer: b) Aerated (Bunsen Type)

Explanation: An aerated burner premixes gas with air (primary air) before combustion using the venturi effect at the injector, producing a hot, blue flame.

4.
A two-stroke engine uses more gasoline and burns a lot of oil, so it is far more
a) Polluting
b) Expensive

Correct Answer: a) Polluting

Explanation: Due to the burning of lubricating oil mixed with fuel and incomplete combustion, 2-stroke engines generally produce more exhaust emissions/pollution than 4-stroke engines.

5.
Which one below is the main activity that takes place in hydro-extractor
a) Spinning, Breaking, Unlocking
b) Washing, Heating, Unlocking
c) Washing, Spinning, Breaking
d) Spinning, Washing, Unblocking
Show Answer

Correct Answer: a) Spinning, Breaking, Unlocking (Most likely Spinning)

8.

Correct Answer: c) Hydro-pneumatic accumulator (or Air Vessel)

9.
The following are components of absorption refrigeration system Except:
a) Evaporation
b) Condenser
c) Reservoirs
d) Filter drier
Show Answer

Correct Answer: d) Filter drier

Explanation: While Evaporation (Evaporator) and Condensers are core parts, "Filter Drier" is a standard component of Vapour Compression systems to protect the compressor/TXV. Absorption systems are sealed chemical loops (Generator/Absorber) and typically don't use standard line filter driers in the same way. The odd one out is usually Compressor (not listed) or Filter Drier.

Which gas law depicts the relationship between the volume of a fixed mass of a gas and its pressure at a constant temperature?

b) Dalton Law

14.
When a piping system is made up primary of friction head and very little of vertical lift then the pump characteristics should be
a) Horizontal
b) Steep
c) First rise and then fall
d) Nearly horizontal
Show Answer

Correct Answer: b) Steep

Explanation: In systems with high friction head (variable with flow squared), a steep pump curve is often preferred because large changes in pressure result in small changes in flow, providing stable control. Flat curves can lead to flow instability in friction-dominated systems if the system curve is shallow.

41.
As the section head of the mechanical workshop in the hospital you are required to oversee proper plumbing that will ensure adequate water supply in the hospital with minimum maintenance. What are some of the key areas to note on the plumbing fixtures and pipes so as ensure good workmanship? (5 marks)
Show Answer
  1. Leak Testing: Ensure all joints (compression, solder, threaded) are watertight under pressure.
  2. Pipe Support: Verify pipes are clipped securely at correct intervals to prevent sagging and vibration (water hammer).
  3. Alignment: Pipes should run parallel/perpendicular to walls; avoid crooked runs.
  4. Accessibility: Ensure stop valves and maintenance points are accessible.
  5. Material Selection: Check correct materials are used (e.g., copper for supply, PVC for waste) and no galvanic corrosion points.
42.
A laundry attendant at Kiambu level 4 hospital complains that the washing machine is not spinning. What could be the probable cause to the problem? (5 marks)
Show Answer
  1. Broken Drive Belt: The belt connecting the motor to the drum may be snapped or slipped.
  2. Faulty Door Interlock: Safety feature preventing spin if door isn't locked detected.
  3. Motor Fault: Worn brushes (if universal motor) or capacitor failure (if induction motor).
  4. Unbalanced Load: Load sensor detected imbalance and aborted spin to prevent damage.
  5. Drainage Issue: Machine won't spin if water hasn't drained (blocked filter/pump).

45.
Explain the functions of the following components in a vapour compression system (5 marks)
i) Compressor
ii) Condenser
iii) Filter drier
iv) Evaporator coil
v) Cold thermostat
Show Answer
  • i) Compressor: Pumps refrigerant, increasing its pressure and temperature to circulate it through the system.
  • ii) Condenser: Rejects heat from the hot refrigerant gas to the surroundings, causing it to condense into a liquid.
  • iii) Filter drier: Removes moisture and solid contaminants from the liquid refrigerant to prevent blockages (ice) and corrosion.
  • iv) Evaporator coil: Absorbs heat from the space being cooled, causing the liquid refrigerant to boil (evaporate) into a gas.
  • v) Cold thermostat: Senses the temperature of the cooled space and switches the compressor on/off to maintain the setpoint.
46.
How is dehumidification achieved by split system in air conditioning? (5 marks)
Show Answer

Dehumidification occurs when the surface temperature of the indoor evaporator coil is maintained below the dew point temperature of the room air. As warm, moist air passes over the cold fins, moisture condenses out of the air into liquid water (condensate). This water drips into a drain pan and is removed via a drain line, lowering the humidity of the air returned to the room.

Section 3: Long Answer Question

20 Marks
49.

a) As the biomedical technician in charge of a level three hospital, the hospital management team sorts for your technical expertise on the purchase of medical refrigerators to be used for the storage of medicines and vaccines in the hospital. What are some of the factors that you should consider for the purchase of these medical refrigerators? (14 marks)

b) What are some of the maintenance carried out on compressor units after every six months? (6 marks)

Show Answer

a) Factors for Purchasing Medical Refrigerators

  1. Temperature Range & Stability: Must maintain specific range (e.g., +2°C to +8°C for vaccines) strictly.
  2. Power Backup/Hold-over Time: Ability to maintain temperature during power outages (Ice-lined refrigerators).
  3. Capacity/Volume: Adequate size for the expected inventory volume.
  4. Alarm Systems: Audio/Visual alarms for high/low temp, door open, and power failure.
  5. Data Logging: Built-in temperature chart recorders or digital loggers for compliance monitoring.
  6. Door Type: Glass (easy viewing, less opening) vs Solid (better insulation). Lockable doors for security.
  7. Climate Class: Rated for the ambient temperature of the location (Tropical rating for hot areas).

b) Compressor Unit Maintenance (6 Monthly)

  1. Clean Condenser: Thoroughly clean dust and lint from condenser fins to maintain efficiency.
  2. Check Electrical Connections: Tighten terminals at the compressor relay and capacitor. Check for burnt wires.
  3. Check Vibration/Mounting: Ensure compressor rubber grommets/mounts are intact to reduce noise/vibration.
  4. Check Amperage: Measure running current and compare with rated specifications.
  5. Inspect for Leaks: Visual check for oil traces on fittings (sign of refrigerant leak).
  6. Listen for Noise: Check for abnormal knocking or grinding sounds indicating internal wear.
