Health Records Management

End Year Examination (Sept 2016 Class)

Section: Essay Questions

1.

a) Describe the concept of scheduling and follow-up

b) Explain the purpose of scheduling and follow-up

Show Answer

Answer:

(a) Concept: Scheduling is the mechanism of controlling patient flow by assigning specific dates/times for attendance to match resources. Follow-up is the system of ensuring patients return for continuing care or tracking those who default.

(b) Purpose: To reduce waiting times, enable file preparation (retrieval) in advance, distribute workload evenly, ensure continuity of care, and prevent clinic overcrowding.

2.

a) Discuss the uses of health records.

b) Explain various function of health records and information department

Show Answer

Answer:

(a) Uses: Patient Care (Primary), Legal Evidence, Research, Education, Billing/Finance, Public Health Monitoring, Hospital Planning.

(b) Functions: Patient Registration, Filing/Storage, Retrieval/Tracing, Coding/Indexing, Statistics Compilation, Medico-legal services, Admission/Discharge procedures.
