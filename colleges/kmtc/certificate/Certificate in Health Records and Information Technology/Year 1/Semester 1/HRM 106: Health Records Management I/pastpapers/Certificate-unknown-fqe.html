Section 3: Long Answer Questions

49. The health records and information department is responsible for filling records and documents in a systematic way and maintaining them in good order in all the hospitals for various purposes

i. Explain the importance of filling medical records (6marks)

ii. Discuss the three major filing methods used in an organized health records department (8marks)

iii. State the advantages of terminal digit filling system over the other filling systems (6marks)

Show Answer

Answer:

(i) Importance: Facilitates easy retrieval, Ensures continuity of care, Prevents loss/damage, Supports research, Legal protection, Saves time.

(ii) Methods: 1. Alphabetical: By name (good for small clinics). 2. Numerical: By number (Straight numeric or Terminal Digit). 3. Chronological: By date (used for registers).

(iii) TDF Advantages: Eliminates misfiling (transposition errors), Even distribution of work, No congestion at end of files, Scalable for large volumes, Enhanced security.
