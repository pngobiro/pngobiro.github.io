Health Records and Information Technology

Duration: Estimated 25 hours Level: College Level

Course Overview

This course provides comprehensive notes on Health Records and Information Technology, as taught at the Kenya Medical Training College. It covers definitions, objectives, history, uses, data collection, analysis, and organizational aspects of health information systems.

ðŸŽ¯ Course Objectives

  • Understand the principles and definitions of Health Information Systems (HIS).
  • Learn about the history, evolution, and goals of HIS.
  • Identify the various components and personnel involved in HIS.
  • Comprehend the different types of data and methods of data collection in healthcare.
  • Analyze the uses of health information at institutional and national levels.
  • Familiarize yourself with different reporting forms and systems within HIS.
  • Grasp the concepts of data analysis, indicators, and interpretation in HIS.
  • Understand the organizational structure of health ministries and the roles within HIS management.
