Module 26: DIRECTOR OF MEDICAL SERVICES (DMS) and CHIEF HEALTH RECORDS &INFORMATION OFFICER (CHRIO)

üéØ Learning Objectives

  • Describe the role and responsibilities of the DIRECTOR OF MEDICAL SERVICES (DMS).
  • Understand the functions of the DMS in program coordination, training, and epidemic monitoring.
  • Describe the role and responsibilities of the CHIEF HEALTH RECORDS &INFORMATION OFFICER (CHRIO).
  • Understand the functions of the CHRIO in policy formulation, coordination, personnel development, and service maintenance.

üìö DIRECTOR OF MEDICAL SERVICES (DMS)

The MDS is the technical adviser of all medical services

  • He coordinates the various programs as per their needs, input and impact e.g. Malaria Control Program, Reproductive Health program etc.
  • He oversees the training in liaison with universities, MTC and other related institutions
  • Monitoring of epidemic from disease surveillance reports, in case of any epidemic he advices the ministry.
  • He also liaises with other health organization e.g. WHO for any input or impact concerning health matters

üìö CHIEF HEALTH RECORDS &INFORMATION OFFICER (CHRIO)

The chief HRIO is the head of HRI management. At this level duties and responsibilities involves:

  • Formulation and implementation of health records and information policies
  • Coordination of HR&I services
  • Development of HRI personnel
  • Advice on implementation of the scheme of service for HRI personnel
  • Maintenance of staff performance appraisal and discipline of HRI services

üìö Key Takeaways

  • The Director of Medical Services (DMS) is the chief technical advisor for all medical services within the Ministry of Health.
  • The DMS coordinates health programs, oversees training with universities and MTC, monitors epidemics through surveillance reports, and advises the ministry on epidemic responses.
  • The DMS also liaises with international health organizations like WHO for inputs and impacts on health matters.
  • The Chief Health Records & Information Officer (CHRIO) heads the Health Records and Information (HRI) management.
  • Key responsibilities of the CHRIO include formulating and implementing HRI policies, coordinating HRI services, developing HRI personnel, advising on service schemes, and maintaining staff performance and discipline within HRI services.
