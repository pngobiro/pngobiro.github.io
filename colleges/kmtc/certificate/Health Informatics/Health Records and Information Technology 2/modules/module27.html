üìö COUNTY HRIO / SUB COUNTY HRIO

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Implementation of administrative policies regarding HRI
  • Planning, organizing, coordinating and controlling health record and information services in all health facilities
  • To plan and conduct in-service training program for HRI personnel and participate in training other personnel in the use of health records and information
  • Participate in teaching duties at medical training college or any other medical institution
  • To serve on HR&I committees and other committees in the health institution
  • To advice on legal aspect of HRI
  • To manage HIS in health institutions and coordinate information from various Sub-Counties

üìö Key Takeaways

  • COUNTY HRIO / SUB COUNTY HRIO are responsible for implementing administrative policies related to Health Records and Information (HRI) at their respective levels.
  • Key duties include planning, organizing, coordinating, and controlling HRI services across all health facilities within their county or sub-county.
  • COUNTY/SUB COUNTY HRIOs are tasked with planning and conducting in-service training for HRI personnel and participating in broader staff training on health records use.
  • They participate in teaching at medical training institutions, serve on relevant committees, advise on legal aspects of HRI, and manage/coordinate HIS within their jurisdiction.
  • The module consolidates key questions for review and discussion, encouraging a comprehensive understanding of the Health Information System.
