üìö ANALYTICAL FORMULAE (continued)

  1. Vacant bed days (VBD) = available bed days ‚Äì(minus) occupied bed days

    VBD = ABD ‚Äì OBD

  2. Available bed days (ABD) = Vacant bed days + Occupied bed days

    ABD = VBD + OBD

  3. Occupied bed days (OBD) = Available bed days ‚Äì Vacant bed days

    OBD = ABD ‚Äì VBD

  4. Length of stay /average length of stay = occupied bed days/ no. of discharges and deaths

    LOS/ALOS/ AV LOS = OBD/D&D

  5. Occupied bed days (OBD) = Length of stay/average length of stay * no. of discharges & deaths

    OBD = LOS/ALOS * D&D

  6. No. of discharges and deaths (D&D) = Occupied bed days/Length of stay

    D&D = OBD/LOS

  7. Turn over per bed / discharges and deaths per available beds = No. of discharges and deaths/no. of available beds

    TOB = D&D/AB

  8. No. of discharges and deaths (D&D) = Turn over per bed * no. of available beds

    D&D = TOB*AB

  9. No. of available beds (AB) = No. of discharges and deaths/Turn over per bed

    AB=D&D/TOB

  10. Turn over interval TOI = Vacant bed days/ no. of discharges and deaths

    TOI = VBD/D&D

  11. Vacant bed days (VBD) = Turn over interval * no. of discharges and deaths

    VBD = TOI * D&D

  12. No. of discharges and deaths (D&D) = Vacant bed days/Turn over interval

    D&D = VBD/TOI

  3. %occupancy

üìö Key Takeaways

  • Vacant Bed Days (VBD) is calculated as Available Bed Days (ABD) minus Occupied Bed Days (OBD).
  • Length of Stay (LOS) is calculated as Occupied Bed Days (OBD) divided by the number of Discharges and Deaths (D&D).
  • Turnover per Bed (TOB) is calculated as Discharges and Deaths (D&D) divided by the number of Available Beds (AB).
  • Turnover Interval (TOI) is calculated as Vacant Bed Days (VBD) divided by the number of Discharges and Deaths (D&D).
  • These formulae are interconnected and used to derive various bed utilization metrics essential for hospital management and planning.
