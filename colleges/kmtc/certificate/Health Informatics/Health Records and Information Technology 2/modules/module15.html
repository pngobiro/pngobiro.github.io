Module 15: Uses of Information at Institutional Level - Part 2

üéØ Learning Objectives

  • Describe the utility of FP summary sheets for institutional planning.
  • Explain the uses of inpatient bed utilization and administration statistics summaries.
  • Understand the applications of surgical operation reports.

üìö 5. FP summary sheet

  • The report will help us know the types and the amount of devices highly required by the community served
  • Help us know of the stuffing and training required
  • Evaluate on the set target and goals in a given period
  • Assess as to whether we achieve the needs of the community
  • Assess the acceptance of the devices as compared to the number of women at child bearing age i.e. 15-49 years

üìö 6. Inpatient bed utilization / administration statistics summary

The summary will assist the facility in its staff allocation as per each ward

  • Budgeting patient food, linen, number of more beds required and other utilities.
  • Determine on facility utilization i.e. is the facility well utilized by the community served
  • Planning for patient admission and the handling of emergency admissions
  • Check whether the bed bureau is well monitored
  • Determine on the quality of services rendered to the patients
  • Estimate on the bed capacity

Inpatient morbidity and mortality summary

This information assists in:

  1. Carrying out research on certain diseases.
  2. Know the trend of common diseases
  3. Know the outcome of common diseases
  4. Drawing a clear picture of the morbidity and mortality pattern of the catchment area
  5. Estimate the amount of various drugs and stationaries needed in the institution
  6. The summary seeks for programs and non-governmental intervention in regard to the epidemic diseases e.g. malaria control program

üìö 7. Surgical operation report

Information from this report for the purpose of:

  1. Estimating the surgical instrument, equipment, drugs etc. needed for theatre management
  2. Staff allocation
  3. Assessing theatre utilization
  4. Organize for good appointment system or waiting list for patients needing operations

üìö Key Takeaways

  • FP summary sheets inform device procurement, staffing, training needs, target achievement, community needs assessment, and device acceptance rates among women of childbearing age.
  • Inpatient bed utilization and administration statistics summaries aid in staff allocation, budgeting for patient needs, facility utilization assessment, admission planning, bed bureau monitoring, service quality determination, and bed capacity estimation.
  • Inpatient morbidity and mortality summaries support disease research, trend analysis, outcome tracking, catchment area morbidity pattern visualization, drug and stationery estimation, and planning for epidemic control programs.
  • Surgical operation reports help in estimating surgical supplies, equipment, and drug needs, staff allocation in theaters, assessing theater utilization, and organizing patient scheduling for surgeries.
