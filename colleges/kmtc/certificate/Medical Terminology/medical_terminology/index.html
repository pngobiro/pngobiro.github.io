Medical Terminology

Duration: 13 hours Level: Beginner

Course Overview

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to medical terminology. You will learn about the basic elements of medical words, including roots, suffixes, and prefixes, and how these combine to create complex medical terms. The course will also cover basic anatomy and physiology and will include details of various body systems to help familiarize you with many specific terms. Through a structured learning path, you'll build the skills needed to understand and use medical language.
