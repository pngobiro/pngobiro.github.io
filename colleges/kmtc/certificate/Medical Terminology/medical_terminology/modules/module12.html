ðŸ“š Medical Word Elements of the Endocrine System

The endocrine system consists of glands that secrete hormones directly into the bloodstream. These hormones travel to cells in specific organs to control their function. The endocrine system includes the pituitary, thyroid, parathyroid, adrenal glands and, in some cases, the pancreas. Here are some of the medical word elements related to this system:

  • Adrenal glands: adren/o
  • Adrenal glands: adrenal/o
  • Calcium: calc/o
  • Secrete: crin/o
  • Sugar, sweetness: gluc/o or glyc/o
  • Homeostasis: home/o
  • Potassium (an electrolyte): kal/i
  • Ketone bodies (acids and acetones): keton/o
  • Night: noct/o
  • Scanty: olig/o
  • Pancreas: pancreat/o
  • Parathyroid glands: parathyroid/o
  • Thymus gland: thym/o

ðŸ“š Pathological Terms

Here are some of the common pathological terms relating to the endocrine system:

  • Addison Disease: A condition caused by a deficiency of adrenal hormones.
  • Cushing Syndrome: A condition caused by excessive production of cortisol.
  • Diabetes mellitus (DM): A metabolic disorder of impaired carbohydrate, protein, and fat metabolism.
  • Diabetes Insipidus: A condition in which the body does not secrete sufficient levels of antidiuretic hormone.
  • Gravesâ€™ Disease: A multisystem autoimmune disorder associated with pronounced hyperthyroidism and exophthalmos.
  • Hypothyroidism: A condition in which the thyroid gland does not produce sufficient levels of thyroid hormone.
  • Hyperthyroidism: A condition characterized by the excessive production of thyroid hormones.
  • Thyroid Carcinoma: A cancer of the thyroid gland.

ðŸ“š Diagnostic, Symptomatic, and Related Terms

Here are some of the more common diagnostic and symptomatic terms relating to the endocrine system:

  • Acromegaly: Chronic metabolic disorder characterized by enlargement of the bones of the face and jaw.
  • Anorexia: Lack or loss of appetite.
  • Hypercalcemia: Excessive amounts of calcium in the blood.
  • Hyperkalemia: Excessive amounts of potassium in the blood.
  • Hyperglycemia: Elevated glucose levels in the blood.
  • Hypoglycemia: Lower than normal glucose levels in the blood.
  • Polyuria: Excessive urination.

ðŸ“š Therapeutic Procedures

Here are some of the most common therapeutic procedures relating to the endocrine system:

  • Parathyroidectomy: Excision of one or more of the parathyroid glands.
  • Thyroidectomy: Excision of the thyroid gland.
  • Subtotal Thyroidectomy: Partial removal of the thyroid gland.
  • Cryosurgery: Technique that exposes abnormal tissue to extreme cold to destroy it.
