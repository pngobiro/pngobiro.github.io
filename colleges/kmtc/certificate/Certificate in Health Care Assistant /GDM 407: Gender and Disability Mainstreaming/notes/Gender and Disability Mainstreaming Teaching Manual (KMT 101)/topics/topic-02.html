üìñ Gender-Based Violence (GBV)

Gender-based violence (GBV) is violence that is directed against a person on the basis of gender. It constitutes a breach of the fundamental right to life, liberty, security, and dignity, equality between women and men, non-discrimination and physical and mental integrity.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Definition

All acts of gender based violence that result in or are likely to result in physical, sexual, psychological or economic harm of suffering to women or men including threats of such acts, coercion or arbitrary, deprivation of liberty whether occurring in public or in private life.

üìã Forms of Gender-Based Violence

Although it is difficult to distinguish between different types of violence since they are not mutually exclusive, gender based violence can be classified as:

üè†

üíî

‚öîÔ∏è

üö´

üì∫

üÜò Response to GBV

A comprehensive response to GBV requires multiple approaches and services:

1

Referral Systems

Establish clear pathways for survivors to access medical, legal, and psychosocial services

2

Safety/Security Measures

Implement protective measures to ensure the safety of survivors and prevent further violence

3

Health Interventions

Provide immediate medical care, post-exposure prophylaxis, and follow-up treatment

4

Psychosocial Support

Offer counseling, trauma-informed care, and support groups for survivors

5

Legal Measures

Ensure access to justice, legal aid, and prosecution of perpetrators

05

üõ°Ô∏è Prevention and Management of GBV

Effective prevention and management strategies include:

1

Assessment/Monitoring

Regular assessment and monitoring of GBV incidents and trends in the community

2

Protection-Sensitive Shelter

Safe shelter and site planning that considers protection needs

3

Distribution and Services

Equitable distribution of resources and services to all community members

4

Safety and Security

Implementation of safety measures and security protocols

5

Raising Community Awareness

Community education and awareness campaigns on GBV prevention

6

Protection Systems

Establishment of community-based protection mechanisms

7

Ethics

Adherence to ethical guidelines in handling GBV cases
