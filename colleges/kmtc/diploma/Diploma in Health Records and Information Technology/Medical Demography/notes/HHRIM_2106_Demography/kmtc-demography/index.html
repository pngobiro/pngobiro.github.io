Analysis and Interpretation of Demography and Social Statistics

HHRIM 2106 | Department of Clinical Medicine

Instructional Manual for HRM - Distance Learning

Course Overview

Module Author: Dr. Mark Mudenyo

This module covers the scientific study of human populations, including size, distribution, structure, and change. It provides a comprehensive guide to demographic measures, data sources, and analytical techniques used in health records and information management.
