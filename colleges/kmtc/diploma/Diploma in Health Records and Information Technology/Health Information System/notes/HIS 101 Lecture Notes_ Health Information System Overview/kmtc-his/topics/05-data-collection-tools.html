Uses of Information Generated at Facility Level

  • Immunization report
    • Monitors and evaluate immunization coverage
    • Monitors and evaluate immunization report
    • Setting of immunization targets
    • Planning for storage facilities
  • Facility workload report
    • Planning e.g. staffing, cost sharing collected expansion/upgrading, funding/resources distribution
    • Determining utilization of the facility

Basic principles of form designing

  1. Is the form necessary?
  2. Simplicity and clarity the form easy to use
  3. Are instructions clear and unambiguous?
  4. Is there any unnecessary duplication with another form?
  5. Is the form economical in terms of paper, printing and the equipment?
  6. Can it be combined with any other for to serve the same purpose?
  7. Is the layout eye catching /attract?
  8. Is font legible?
  9. Remember to pre test
