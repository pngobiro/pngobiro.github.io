About FQE Papers

  • These papers are administered by KNEC/KMTC at the end of the Diploma course.
  • They typically consist of Section 1 (MCQs), Section 2 (Short Answer), and Section 3 (Long Answer/Essay).
  • Standard duration is 3 hours.
