Definition of Terms

  • Data: Is raw facts/real world observations, events, transactions and so on which have been recorded.
  • Information: Is processed data for use
  • Knowledge: Contextual, relevant, actionable information.
  • Wisdom: Sapience or sagacity is the ability to think and act using knowledge, experience, understanding, common sense and insights. The collection of one's knowledge.
  • Health: Is a state of wellbeing physically, mentally and socially and not merely absence of diseases or infirmity (WHO, 1948).
  • Health information: is information about people's health status, resources, activities and events as related to health.
  • Informatics: is the science of how to use data, information and knowledge to improve human health and the delivery of health care services.
  • Health Informatics: Is the integration of knowledge from informatics, healthcare, health information technology and other disciplines to analyze and protect patient data, increase healthcare efficiencies and produce higher quality patient care and enhance research.
  • System: A collection of components that work together in a regular or orderly manner, to achieve a common objective.
  • A health system: Consists of all organizations, people and actions whose primary intent is to promote, restore or maintain health.
  • Information System (IS): Is an integrated set of components for collecting, processing and storing data and for delivering information, knowledge, and digital products. OR The entire infrastructure that collect, process, store, analyze and distribute information for a dedicated purpose.

Further Definitions and Roles

Description of health information Management:

Is the practice of acquiring, analyzing and protecting digital and traditional medical information vital in providing quality patient care.

It refers to any system that captures, stores manages or transmits information related to the health of individuals or the activities or organizations that work within health sector.

What is the district health information system/softwares.

The District health information software (DHIS) is used in more than 40 countries around the world. DHIS is open source software platform for reporting analysis and dissemination of data for health programs developed by the health information system program in the University of Oslo Norway. (HISP).

What is health information professional?

They specialize in managing patient health information and medical records, information system and classifying using standard coding system, the diagnosis and procedures for health care services provided to patients.

What is the role of Health information technician?

Description of compiling ,process and maintain medical records of hospital and clinic patients in a manner consistent with medical, administrative, ethical, legal and regulatory requirements of the health care system.

What is a patient information clerk

Duties and responsibilities is responsible for compiling, coding, organizing and evaluating patient medical records

What is health information technology?

It is a broad concept that encompasses an array of technology to store, share and analyze health information.
