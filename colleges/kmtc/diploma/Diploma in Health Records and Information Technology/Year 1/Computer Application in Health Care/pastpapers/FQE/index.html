About FQE Papers

  • Theory Papers: Usually consist of Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) and Short Answer Questions covering database concepts, networking, and algorithms.
  • Practical Papers: Hands-on tasks involving MS Access (Database creation, Queries, Forms), MS Excel (Formulas, Analysis), MS PowerPoint, and basic HTML Web Design.
  • Duration: Typically 3 hours for Practical and 45 mins - 3 hours for Theory.
