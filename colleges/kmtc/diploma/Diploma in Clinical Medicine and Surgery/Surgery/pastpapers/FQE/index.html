About FQE Papers

  • Final Qualifying Examinations are the licensure exams for Clinical Officers.
  • Standard format typically includes Section 1 (MCQs), Section 2 (Short Answer), and Section 3 (Long Answer).
  • Duration is usually 3 hours.
