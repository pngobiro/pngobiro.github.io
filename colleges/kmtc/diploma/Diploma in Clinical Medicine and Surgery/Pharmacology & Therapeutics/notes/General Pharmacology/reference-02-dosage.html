Artemether-Lumefantrine (AL) Dosing

First Line for Uncomplicated Malaria. Dosing schedule: 0h, 8h, 24h, 36h, 48h, 60h (Total 6 doses).

Weight (kg) Age (Approx) Tablets per Dose Total Tabs
5 - 14 kg 5mo - 3 yrs 1 6
15 - 24 kg 3 - 7 yrs 2 12
25 - 34 kg 8 - 11 yrs 3 18
> 34 kg > 12 yrs / Adult 4 24

Severe Malaria: Quinine Protocol

  • 1
    Loading Dose: 20 mg/kg body weight in 5% dextrose (run over 4 hours).
  • 2
    Maintenance Dose: 10 mg/kg body weight every 8 hours (starting 8 hours after loading dose).
  • Switch to oral medication as soon as patient can tolerate. Total treatment: 7 days.
