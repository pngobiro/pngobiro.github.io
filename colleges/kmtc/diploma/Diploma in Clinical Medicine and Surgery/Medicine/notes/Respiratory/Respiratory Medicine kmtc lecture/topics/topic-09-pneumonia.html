PNEUMOCYSTS CARINII PNEUMOMA

Caused by pneumocystis carinii accounts for 50% of cases with HIV / AIDS and CD4 counts < 200/mm3

PATHOLOGY

It damages the alveolar epithelium which impedes gas exchange and reduces lung compliance

CLINICAL PRESENTATION

  1. Subacute and insidious onset over a period of weeks with prolonged period of increasing shortness of breath usually on exercution
  2. Non productive cough
  3. Low grade fever and malaise

OIE

Tachypnoea, Tachycardia cyanosis hypora

RS ‚Äì Fine crackles although in mild cases no auscultatory abnormality

DIAGNOSIS

  1. CXR- Early X- Rays shows no abnormality. Later will show diffuse bilateral alveolar and interstitial shadowing beginning in the perihilar regions and spreading out in butterfly pattern.

    may also show localized infiltration nodules cavitations or pneumothorax.
  2. High resolution ct scan ‚Äì demonstrate characteristic ground appearance
  3. Induced sputum. Bronchio alveolar lavage. High lactate dehydrogenase

    IV septin 120 mg IV 3-4 divided doses x 21 days

  2. If hypoxic cyanosis oxygen

    40mg BID 5/7 Then 20 mg od x 11/7
  4. Secondary prophylaxis Septin 960mg od
  5. Prophylaxis . 1st choice septin 960mg od. 2nd choice Dupsone 100mg od.
