Nutrient Broth

Nutrient broth forms the basis of most culture media. There are three types: meat infusion, digest, and meat extract broth.

Meat Infusion Broth

Watery extract of lean meat (fat-free) infused at 4¬∞C.

  • Lean meat: 500 g
  • Water: 1000 ml
  • Peptone: 10‚Äì12 g
  • NaCl: 5 g

Prep: Infuse 24 hrs. Boil/Steam. Filter. Add peptone/salt. Adjust pH 7.5. Autoclave 121¬∞C/15 min.

Meat Extract Broth

Prepared from commercial extract (Lab-Lemco).

  • Meat extract: 10 g
  • Peptone: 10 g
  • NaCl: 5 g
  • Water: 1000 ml

Prep: Dissolve by heat. Adjust pH 7.5. Filter. Autoclave 115¬∞C/15 min.

Digest Broth

Enzyme-digested meat broth (using Trypsin/Pancreatic extract). Supports luxuriant growth of haemolytic streptococci.

Peptone Water

Used for indole testing and sugar fermentation bases.

  • Peptone: 2 g
  • NaCl: 1 g
  • Water: 200 ml

Prep: Dissolve, adjust pH 7.4. Dispense. Autoclave 121¬∞C/15 min.

Nutrient Agar

Nutrient broth + 1‚Äì1.5% Agar powder. Used for subculture and as a base for enriched media.

Blood Agar

Formula: Nutrient agar or Columbia blood agar base + 5% Defibrinated blood (Horse/Sheep).

üìù

Preparation

  1. 1Sterilise base at 121¬∞C for 15 min.
  2. 2Cool to 50¬∞C.
  3. 3Add 5% sterile blood aseptically.
  4. 4Mix gently and pour plates.

Chocolate Agar

Same as blood agar but heated to release X and V factors (for Haemophilus and Neisseria).

Prep: Add blood to base. Heat in water bath at 75¬∞C (or steamer) until chocolate colour (10-15 mins). Cool to 50¬∞C and pour.

üéØ Selective Media: Respiratory & Genital

For Corynebacterium diphtheriae

Hoyle's Medium

Blood agar base + Laked horse blood + Potassium tellurite.

  • Base: 100 ml
  • Laked blood: 5 ml
  • 3.5% Potassium tellurite: 1 ml

Loeffler's Serum Slope

Ox serum (3 parts) + 1% Glucose broth (1 part). Inspissate at 75¬∞C.

Elek's Medium: For toxin production. Proteose peptone, maltose, lactic acid, serum.

For Bordetella pertussis

Bordet-Gengou Medium: Potato extract + glycerol + blood + cephalexin.

Charcoal Cephalexin Blood Agar (CCBA): Charcoal agar + blood + cephalexin (40mg/l). Better shelf life.

For Neisseria gonorrhoeae

Modified Thayer-Martin (MTM): GC Agar base + Haemoglobin + GC Supplement (VCNT: Vancomycin, Colistin, Nystatin, Trimethoprim).

Modified New York City (MNYC): GC base + Laked horse blood + VCAT/LCAT supplement (Lincomycin instead of Vancomycin).

04

ü¶† Enteric Culture Media

MacConkey Agar

Differential (Lactose) and mildly selective (Bile salts).

  • Lactose fermenters: Red colonies (Acid + Neutral Red).
  • Non-lactose fermenters: Pale colonies.
  • Swarming: Inhibited by omitting NaCl.

Deoxycholate Citrate Agar (DCA)

Selective for Salmonella and Shigella. Contains sodium deoxycholate, sodium citrate, ferric citrate (for $H_2S$). Heat sensitive - do not overheat.

CLED Medium

Cystine-Lactose-Electrolyte Deficient. Prevents Proteus swarming. Used for urine. Indicator: Bromothymol blue (Yellow = Acid/LF, Blue = NLF).

Selenite F Broth

Enrichment for Salmonella. Sodium biselenite inhibits coliforms.
Warning: Sodium selenite is teratogenic. Do not autoclave (Steam only).

TCBS Medium

Thiosulphate Citrate Bile Salt Sucrose. Selective for Vibrio.
V. cholerae: Yellow colonies (Sucrose +).
V. parahaemolyticus: Green colonies (Sucrose -).

05

üè∫ Anaerobic Media

Robertson's Cooked Meat (RCM)

Meat particles provide reducing substances (glutathione).
Saccharolytic: Meat turns pink (acid).
Proteolytic: Meat turns black (digestion/foul odour).

Thioglycollate Broth

Contains sodium thioglycollate (reducing agent) and resazurin (red = oxidised, colourless = reduced).

Lactose-Egg-Yolk-Milk Agar

Differential for Clostridia. Detects: Lactose fermentation (red colonies), Lecithinase (opacity), Lipase (pearly layer), Proteolysis (clearing).

06

üî¨ Mycobacterial Media

Lowenstein-Jensen (LJ) Medium

Inspissated egg medium.

  • Ingredients: Eggs, Asparagine, Glycerol (or Pyruvate), Malachite Green (inhibitor).
  • Glycerol LJ: For M. tuberculosis (Eugonic).
  • Pyruvate LJ: For M. bovis (Dysgonic - inhibited by glycerol).
  • Preparation: Inspissate at 75¬∞C until solid.

Middlebrook 7H10 Agar

Synthetic medium. Requires OADC enrichment (Oleic acid, Albumin, Dextrose, Catalase). Store in dark (light produces toxic formaldehyde).

07

üß™ Other Special Media

Sabouraud Dextrose Agar (SDA)
For Fungi. Acid pH (5.5). Can add Chloramphenicol/Cycloheximide for selectivity.
Campylobacter Media
Blood agar + Selective Supplements (Skirrow, Butzler, or Blaser-Wang). Contain Vancomycin, Polymyxin B, Trimethoprim. Incubate at 42¬∞C.
Legionella CYE Agar
Charcoal Yeast Extract. Buffered. Requires Cysteine and Iron (growth supplement) + Antibiotics (selective).
Mannitol Salt Agar
7.5% Salt (Selects Staph) + Mannitol + Phenol Red. S. aureus = Yellow colonies.
Christensen's Urea
Detects Urease. Pink = Positive. (Proteus, H. pylori).
