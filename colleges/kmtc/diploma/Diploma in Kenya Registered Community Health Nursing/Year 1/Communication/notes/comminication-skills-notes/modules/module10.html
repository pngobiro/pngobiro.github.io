Computer technology and telecommunication links via satellite are bringing about revolution changes in the communication field, collapsing the barriers of space and time.

Long time it took a long time even days to communicate to someone through letters but due to technological improvements communication has become an instantaneous process.

As none organization are becoming information based there is need for effective knowledge by students studying communication skills, as many organizations are looking it improve their business process with an aim of being effective, efficient and competitive.

Office automation is synonymously referred to as elements office which is a combination of various technologies meant to improve efficiency of office work and also reduce the number of office staff under employment.

Office automation system that collect, process, store and transmit electronics messages, documents and other forms of communication among individual, work groups and organizations.

Such systems improve and increase productivity of managerial users and often professionals by significantly reducing the time and effort needed to produce access and receive business communication.

1. WORD PROCESSOR

It combines in it the characteristics of a computer and a type written and can greatly simplify the work of a written communication.

As one type on a word processer the texts appear on the screen and we can edit, revise, the texts as we like.

  • We can add or delete words, phrases or sentences wherever we like.
  • We can more the whole text from one point to another.
  • We can easily locate where a word or phrase appears in the text for the first time or it occur again and we can replace it.
  • Spell check programs are also available and they check every word if correctly spoiled and this help in eliminating wrongly spelled words and phrases.
  • The system can be instructed to produce top, bottom right and left margins.
  • It can center headings on a page
  • There are different type faces available and there for one can type different parts in different faces to emphasis them and main the page interacting.
  • If our texts run into the next page, the system can automatically put heading and page number on each page.

TELEX

Telex is the name given by post office people to a teleprompter where by written messages can be communicated from one place to another with the help of machine and it consists of parts.

  • Key board transmitter
  • Receiver four transmitting the coded signals and printing the massage.

When a message is to be sent, the typist presses a button, waits for the for the dial tone, dial the number desired and if the number is contacted types the message and sent typed on a small strip of a paper on the received end.

Advantages of telex services

It enables transmission of written messages from one subscriber to another by dialing. If combines in it an advantage of both telephone and writing a letter. Since telex messages are change according to the number of words contained in them they have to be composed like a telegram which means they should be brief but complete and clear.
