Business Letter

There are many different reasons for writing business letter, however most business letter follow some general as described below;

  • Use block style i.e. does not event paragraphs.
  • Include the address of the person you are writing to at the top of the letter below your company letter.
  • After the address double the space and include date.
  • You double the space to put the body of the letter in the center.
  • Before you write include the salutation and different reasons.
  • In the body state the reason for writing and reference.
  • You can make any request you have within.
  • If there is to be further contact then refer to this contact e.g. I look forward to meeting you.
  • Close the letter with thank you.
  • Then finish the letter with salutation e.g. yours faithfully.
  • Double space and type your full name and title.
  • Sign the letter between the salutation and the typed name.

Phrases used in any standard business letter.

At the start use dear sir/madam if you don't know whom you are writing to.

Use dear doctor, Mr., Mrs., miss, ms and the name of the person if you know who you are writing to and have a formal relationship with.

Use dear and the name of that person if the person is a close business contact and or a friend.

The reference with reference to your advertisement in the daily nation on 23 match 2009 or with reference to your letter on 13 June or with reference to your call this morning...............

Reason for writing

Writing to inquire about e.g. is writing to apologize, confirmation meeting letter sent etc.

Requesting

  • Could you possibly send or ...........
  • Could you please avail yourself for the interview?
  • Kindly confirm if ...........

Agreeing to request

I would be delighted to..........

Phrases of giving bad news in business letters

  • Unfortunately
  • I am afraid that.........
  • I regret to inform you that...........

Phrases when enclosing document

  • I am enclosing..............
  • Please find enclosed copies of........
  • Enclosed you will find my testimonials........
  • I have hereby attached the copy of...........

Closing remarks

  • Thank you for...........
  • Please contact us..........

Reference to the future

I look forward to.........

The finish

Use yours faithfully, if you don't know the name of the person you are writing to.

Use your sincerely if you know the name of the person.

Use best regards, or best wishes if the person is a close business contacts or friends.

How to write a job application letter

Application letter are probably one of the most important documents you will ever write in your life

This is sometimes referred to as ‚Äúcover letter‚Äù and is normally accompanied by a resume. What should be born in mind is that this letter is what sells you to the employer.

It's like a product which is well researched , advertised promoted, priced, distributed, and added value so that it can attract many clients as possible with the hope that one will eventually buy it.

The down side of it is that, there will be quite a number of people will be jostling for the same position and the competition will be stiff to the point of retrace.

The struggle goes down to survival for the fittest and defiantly you will need some survival skills to make it.

Ask yourself what makes me to stand out of the crowd to convince this employer that I have what it takes to do this job? Why should the employer hire you than someone else?

The employer on the other hand when looking at your letter is asking himself; what is it for me? How can this person be of value to me? What do I stand to gain if I hire him or her?

Application are not written to get the job rather it is to convince the employer to give you an interview where you will get a better opportunity to sell yourself more and even better, before the employer makes a decision to here you or not.

In this letter you must therefore makes a good imprison of yourself

Make your letter strong enough to convince your potential employer that your distinctive background qualifies you for the job, but the letter should not be long that may turn your readers off.

Remember to use miniskirts analogy the letter just like a miniskirt; it should be short enough to arouse interest but long enough to cover the subject.

Present the details that you would want to make your reader to want to keep reading it.

The letter should not be more than a page long, in the length balance your words to fit in the length.

Be profession, make sure your letter is professional I formal, style, grammar.

Maintain a cautious positive bright time throughout the letter and estimate all errors.

Remember that the readers/ employer often deselect the applicants because of the appearance of the letter.

First for the purpose of introduction it's important to obtain a name for your salutation if you don't have one. For example you might call the company and ask who receives the application instead of generally addressing the letter as dear sir/madam.

The kind of work your organization is involved particularly interest me. It would be helpful to conduct a reconnaissance study learn as much as possible about the people you want to work for.
