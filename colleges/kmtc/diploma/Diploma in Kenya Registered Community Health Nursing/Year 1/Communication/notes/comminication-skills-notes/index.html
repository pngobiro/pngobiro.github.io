Communication Skills Notes

Duration: 10 hours Level: Intermediate

Course Overview

This course provides a comprehensive overview of communication skills necessary for both personal and professional success. It covers various aspects, from the basic elements of communication to advanced techniques in writing, public speaking, and interviewing.

  • Master techniques in public speaking and interviewing.

Course Modules

  1. Introduction to Communication Skills

    An overview of basic concepts, social, business communication and key communication elements.

    90 minutes

  2. Levels of Communication & The 7 C's

    Explore the different levels of communication and the principles for effective communication.

    120 minutes

  3. Types of Communication

    Discussing internal and external communication flow, including verbal, non-verbal, and written communication.

    120 minutes

  4. Barriers to Effective Communication

    Understanding the various barriers that hinder effective communication and how to overcome them.

    120 minutes

  5. Speech, Listening, Reports, Letters, and CVs

    Techniques in public speaking, effective listening, and essential writing skills, including report and CV development.

    150 minutes

Prerequisites

  • Basic understanding of English.
  • A willingness to improve communication skills.
