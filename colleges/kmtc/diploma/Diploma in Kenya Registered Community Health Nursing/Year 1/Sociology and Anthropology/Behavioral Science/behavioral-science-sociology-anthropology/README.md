## ğŸ“– Course Content

### Module Topics Covered

1. **Basic Concepts and Terminology**
   - Definition of sociology and anthropology
   - Key terminologies and concepts
   - Historical perspectives

2. **Founding Fathers of Sociology**
   - Auguste Comte (1798-1857)
   - Karl Marx (1818-1883)
   - Max Weber (1864-1920)
   - Emile Durkheim (1858-1917)

3. **Man and Culture**
   - Relationship between individual and society
   - Social contract theory vs. organismic theory
   - Cultural beliefs and practices

4. **Socialization Process**
   - Primary and secondary socialization
   - Agents of socialization
   - Types and phases of socialization

5. **Social Institutions**
   - Family structures and functions
   - Marriage institutions
   - Educational, political, economic, and religious institutions
   - Healthcare institutions

6. **Social Stratification and Mobility**
   - Class systems and social hierarchy
   - Factors affecting social mobility
   - Social mobilization concepts

7. **Social Change**
   - Theories of social change
   - Types of change (evolution, revolution, reform)
   - Rural-urban migration effects

8. **Conflict Resolution and Peace Building**
   - Types and causes of conflicts
   - Conflict resolution techniques
   - Negotiation processes

9. **Counseling in Healthcare**
   - Principles and types of counseling
   - Counseling techniques and approaches
   - Application in healthcare settings
