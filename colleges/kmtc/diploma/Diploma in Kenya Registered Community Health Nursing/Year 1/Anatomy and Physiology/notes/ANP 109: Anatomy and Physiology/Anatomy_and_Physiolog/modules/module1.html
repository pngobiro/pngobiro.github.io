üéØ Learning Objectives

  • Define science and its role in understanding the human body.

üìö Introduction

Science is the method of observing and measuring natural phenomena in order to explain them. Observation, experimentation, imagination, and time have led to tremendous advances in the understanding of the human body and how it functions.

Form and Function are inextricably linked.

Human Anatomy: The study of the structure of the human body.

Human Physiology: The study of the body's functions.

üìù Summary

  • Science involves observation, measurement, and experimentation to explain natural phenomena.
  • Human anatomy is the study of the body's structure.
  • Human physiology is the study of the body's functions.
  • Form and function are closely related in the human body.
