Labour Force Survey - 1998/99

  • There were $15.9$ million persons aged $15-64$ of the working population. 77.4 percent of this population was economically active.
  • Among the active population were youth between 22-34 years of age.
  • Out of the working population the youth aged between 15-19 years employed were 875000, unemployed 312500 and inactive 2375000.
  • The overall labour force participation rate for the population aged 15-64 years was 73.6 percent.
  • Males participation rate was 74.7 percent while female participation rate was 72.6 percent.
  • The male youth aged 15-19 years participation rate was 77 percent while female youth aged $15-19$ years was 30 percent.
