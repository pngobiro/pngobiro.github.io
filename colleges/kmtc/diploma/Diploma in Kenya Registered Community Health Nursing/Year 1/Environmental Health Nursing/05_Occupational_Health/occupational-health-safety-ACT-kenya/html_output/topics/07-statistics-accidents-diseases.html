  • The Factories and Other Places of Work Act requires that where an accident in a factory premises, place of work or location
    1. Causes loss of life
    2. Disables any person fro more than three days, a written notice of the accident should be sent to an OHSO and in case of a fatal accident within and not later than twenty-four hours of its occurrence.
