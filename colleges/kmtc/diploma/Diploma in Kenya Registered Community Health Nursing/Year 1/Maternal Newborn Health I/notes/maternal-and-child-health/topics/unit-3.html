The first year, particularly the first six months, is the most critical period of development in a child's life. While growth may slow after infancy and pick up again in adolescence, developmental changes remain rapid and crucial throughout childhood.

Growth vs Development

Growth
The increase in weight and height with age, resulting from cell multiplication and differentiation.
Development
The process of maturation and advancement, involving qualitative improvements in capabilities and skills.
