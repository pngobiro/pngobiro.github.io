Health Education AND Promotion

Duration: 6 hours Level: Intermediate

Course Overview

This course is designed to introduce learners to the fundamental principles of health education and health promotion. It explores various strategies and methods used in health education, emphasizing the importance of planning, implementation, and evaluation in creating effective health programs. The course also delves into different teaching methods and the use of teaching aids and instructional materials in enhancing the learning experience in health education.
