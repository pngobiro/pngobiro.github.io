Conclusion

After following these 6 steps, the outline of the M&E plan should look something like this:

  • -Introduction to program
  • Program goals and objectives
  • -Logic model/Logical Framework/Theory of change
  • Indicators
  • -Table with data sources, collection timing, and staff member responsible
  • -Roles and Responsibilities
  • -Description of each staff member's role in M&E data collection, analysis, and/or reporting
  • Reporting
  • -Analysis plan
  • -Reporting template table
  • -Dissemination plan
  • -Description of how and when M&E data will be disseminated internally and externally

Finally

üìö Key Takeaways

  • Developing a comprehensive M&E plan involves six key steps, from identifying program goals to planning dissemination.
  • A complete M&E plan includes an introduction, program logic, indicators, data management, roles, reporting, analysis, and dissemination strategies.
  • Following these steps ensures a structured approach to monitoring and evaluating program effectiveness and impact.
