Who should develop a Monitoring and Evaluation Plan?

An M&E plan should be developed by the research team or staff with research experience, with inputs from program staff involved in designing and implementing the program.

üìö When should a Monitoring and Evaluation Plan be developed? & Steps for monitoring and evaluation

When should a Monitoring and Evaluation Plan be developed?

An M&E plan should be developed at the beginning of the program when the interventions are being designed. This will ensure there is a system in place to monitor the program and evaluate success.

Steps for monitoring and evaluation

üìö Key Takeaways

  • An M&E plan should be developed collaboratively by research staff and program staff to ensure expertise and practical insights are included.
  • The M&E plan should be created at the very beginning of the program, during the intervention design phase, to proactively establish monitoring and evaluation systems.
  • The module will next introduce the specific steps involved in the monitoring and evaluation process.
