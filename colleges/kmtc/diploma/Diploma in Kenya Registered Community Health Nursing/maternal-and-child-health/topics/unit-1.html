Unit 1

Introduction to Maternal and Child Nutrition

🎯 Unit Objectives

By the end of this unit, you will be able to:

  • • Define key terms used in maternal and child nutrition
  • • Understand fundamental concepts of maternal and child nutrition

📚 Key Terms and Definitions

Conception
The fertilization of an ovum by a sperm, marking the beginning of pregnancy.
Zygote
A fertilized ovum that undergoes cell division and becomes implanted in the uterus.
Embryo
The developing organism from conception to the eighth week, featuring a complete central nervous system, beating heart, and other vital organs.
Foetus
The developing baby from the end of the embryo stage until birth, when organs grow to maturity.
Gestation
The period from conception to birth (38-42 weeks), divided into three trimesters.
