Introduction to Gynaecology

GYNAECOLOGY

Def: It is the science dealing with the diseases of the female reproductive system.

Broad objective (Outcome)

To promote health, prevent illness and complications, assess diagnose, manage and rehabilitate women with common gynecological problems.

Specific objectives (learning outcomes)

By the end of the course, the learner will be able to;

  1. 1.Define gynecological terms
  2. 2.List, state and describe the causes, predisposing factors, clinical features, prognosis, total management and complications of gynecological disorders
  3. 3.Obtain history from women with gynecological problems
  4. 4.Plan and implement nursing care of patients suffering from gynecological conditions
  5. 5.Share health massage with the public on gynecological problems in order to prevent, rehabilitate and support women with gynecological problems

Content
