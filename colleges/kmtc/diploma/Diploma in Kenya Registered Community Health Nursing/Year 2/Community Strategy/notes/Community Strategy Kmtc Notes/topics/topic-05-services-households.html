ü©∫ Services Offered at Level One

Level One services focus on preventive and promotive health care delivered directly to the community. These include:

  • Disease Prevention & Control

    Activities aimed at reducing morbidity, disability, and mortality.

  • Family Health Services

    Expanding family planning, maternal, child, and youth services.

  • Hygiene & Environmental Sanitation

    Promoting clean living environments and safe practices.

  • Health Promotion

    Educating communities to empower them to take charge of their health.

  • Referrals

    Connecting community members to health facilities for better management of conditions.

üõ°Ô∏è Household Responsibilities: Disease Prevention

To prevent disease, households are responsible for:

  • Hygiene: Practicing good personal hygiene (e.g., washing hands, using latrines).
  • Water Safety: Using safe drinking water.
  • Shelter & Vectors: Ensuring adequate shelter and protection against vectors of disease.
  • Safety: Preventing accidents and abuse, and taking appropriate action when they occur.
  • Sexual Health: Ensuring appropriate sexual behavior to prevent transmission of STIs.
