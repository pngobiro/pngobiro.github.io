üöë Referral Mechanisms

Referral is defined as the act of sending a person to a link health facility for further management. It is a critical component for the continuum of care and uptake of services (like LAPM).

The Referral Process

  • 1. Screening & Decision

    Contact with the patient: Screening is completed and a decision for referral is made.

  • 2. Filling the Form

    Completing the referral form with patient data, reasons for referral, and CHV details.

  • 3. Action & Feedback

    Action taken by the receiving officer and feedback sent back to the community.

üìù Community Referral Form

The MOH 100 form is used to refer patients to the health facility. It communicates necessary information to health professionals and provides a mechanism for feedback to the CHV for follow-up.

Form Structure

SECTION A: Referral to Facility
Patient Data Date, Time, Name, Age, Sex, Name of Community Unit.
Reason for Referral Main problem, Treatment given, Comments.
Referring CHV Name, Mobile No, Village/Estate, Sub-location, Location.

Feedback Name of CHV, Mobile No, "Call made by referring officer?" (Yes/No), Instructions ("Kindly do the following to the patient...").

üìä Community Based Health Information System (CBHIS)

A Community-Based Health Information System (CBHIS) is a dynamic system that includes information on how data are collected, how they flow, how to assess and improve their quality, and how they are used. It involves the collection, management, and analysis of data on health and related services provided to communities outside of facilities.

Process of Setting Up a CBHIS

  1. 1 Develop Framework: Define goals, objectives, stakeholders, end users, and key performance indicators.
  2. 2 Landscape Analysis: Analyze existing paper and digital systems that need to interoperate.
  3. 3 Assessment: Assess stakeholder and user information needs.
  4. 4 Solution Choice: Choose the solution (paper or digital) and develop a plan for scale and sustainability.
  5. 5 Design: Map work/data flows, storyboard the system, and refine based on feedback. Build security protocols.
  6. 6 Test & Deploy: Develop a prototype, test with users, refine, and deploy the system.
