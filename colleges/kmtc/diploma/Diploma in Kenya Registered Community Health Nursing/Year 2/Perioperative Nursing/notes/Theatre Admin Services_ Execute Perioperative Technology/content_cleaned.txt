<li>Division of Work</li>

<p>Henri believed that segregating work in the workforce amongst the workers will enhance the quality of the product. Similarly, he also concluded that the division of work improves the productivity, efficiency, accuracy and speed of the workers. This principle is appropriate for both the managerial as well as a technical work level.</p>

<li>Authority and Responsibility</li>

<ol start="3">
<li>Discipline</li>
</ol>
<p>Without discipline, nothing can be accomplished. It is the core value for any project or any management. Good performance and sensible interrelation make the management job easy and comprehensive. <li>Unity of Direction</li>
