üîÑ Process of Carcinogenesis

Three Steps of Carcinogenesis

  1. INITIATION: The first stage where normal cells are exposed to a carcinogen.
  2. PROMOTION: The second stage where agents (promoters) enhance the growth of initiated cells.
  3. PROGRESSION: The final stage where the tumor cells acquire malignant characteristics and changes.
