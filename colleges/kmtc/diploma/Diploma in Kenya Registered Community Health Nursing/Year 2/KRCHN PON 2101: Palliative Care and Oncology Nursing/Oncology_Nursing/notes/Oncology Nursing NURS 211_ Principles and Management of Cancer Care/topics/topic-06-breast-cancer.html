Most common cancer in women.

Risk Factors

Physical Changes

  • Painless lump in the breast or underarm
  • Flattening or indentation on the breast
  • Change in the size, contour, texture of the breast
  • Pitted surface like the skin of an orange (Peau d'orange)

Nipple Changes

  • Indrawn or inverted
  • Dimpled
  • Unusual discharge (Clear, Bloody, or any color)
