Infection Prevention and Control

Duration: 10 hours Level: Intermediate

Course Overview

This course provides a comprehensive overview of infection prevention and control principles and practices relevant to community health nursing. It covers various aspects, including infectious disease concepts, risk factors, modes of transmission, standard precautions, sterilization techniques, waste management, and barrier nursing guidelines.
