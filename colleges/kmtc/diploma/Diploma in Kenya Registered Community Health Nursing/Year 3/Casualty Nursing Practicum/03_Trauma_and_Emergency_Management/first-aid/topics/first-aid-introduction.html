Introduction & Principles

Objectives

By the end of the lesson the learner should be able to;

  1. define relevant terms
  2. discuss process of administering first aid and Priority Emergency Measures for

All Patients

  • Make safety the first priority
  • Preplan to ensure security and a safe environment
  • Closely observe patient

Patient and family-focused interventions

  • Relieve anxiety and provide a sense of security
  • Allow family to stay with patient, if possible, to alleviate anxiety
  • Provide explanations and information
  • Provide additional interventions depending upon the stage of crisis

Common Trauma

  • Burns
  • Heat
  • Bites/stings
  • Cold
  • Electrical
  • Altitude
  • Near drowning
  • Spinal
  • Head injury
  • Musculoskeletal
  • Stab/gunshot wounds
  • Rape
