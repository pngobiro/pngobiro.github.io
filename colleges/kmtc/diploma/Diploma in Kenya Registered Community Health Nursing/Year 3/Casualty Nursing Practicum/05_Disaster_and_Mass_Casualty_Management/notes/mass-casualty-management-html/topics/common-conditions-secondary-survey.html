Common Life Threatening Conditions

BreathingHaemothorax

Secondary Survey

  • •Secondary survey is undertaken when the patient's ABCs are stable
  • •To identify all minor injuries missed in primary survey
  • •If any deterioration occurs must be interrupted by Primary survey.
  • •Patient History
  • •Head to Toe examination
  • •Complete neurological examination
  • •Diagnostic Tests
  • •Re-evaluation

Patient History

  • •A - Allergies
  • •M - Medication currently used
  • •P - Pregnancy / Past Illness
  • •L - Last Meal
  • •E - Events
