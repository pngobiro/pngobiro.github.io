Characteristics of Disaster

Characteristics of Disaster

  1. 1.The normal function of a community is disrupted.
  2. 2.Disaster exceed the coping mechanism of community.
  3. 3.The disruption is of such magnitude that there is an inability to return to normal functioning without external assistance.
