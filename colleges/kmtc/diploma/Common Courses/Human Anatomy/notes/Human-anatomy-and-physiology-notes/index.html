Marri Laxman Reddy Institute of Pharmacy

Human Anatomy and Physiology

Comprehensive Study Notes for Pharmacy Students

4 Units • 10 Topics • Full Course Coverage

Course Topics

01

Introduction to Anatomy

Definition, scope, levels of structural organization, body systems, and homeostasis.

 02

The Cell

Structure, functions, organelles, transport mechanisms, and cell division.

 03

Tissues

Classification, structure, and function of Epithelial, Connective, Muscle, and Nervous tissues.

 04

Integumentary System

Structure and function of the skin, epidermis layers, and accessory structures.

 05

Skeletal System & Joints

Bone types, axial vs appendicular skeleton, and classification of joints.

 06

Nervous System Basics

Organization of the NS, neuron structure, neuroglia, and action potentials.

 07

Central Nervous System

Brain structure, meninges, ventricles, spinal cord anatomy, and reflexes.

 08

Peripheral Nervous System

Cranial nerves, spinal nerves, and the Autonomic Nervous System (Sympathetic/Parasympathetic).

 09

Special Sense Organs

Anatomy and physiology of the Eye, Ear, Nose, and Tongue.

 10

Endocrine System

Hormones, Pituitary, Thyroid, Parathyroid, Adrenal, Pancreas, and Pineal glands.
