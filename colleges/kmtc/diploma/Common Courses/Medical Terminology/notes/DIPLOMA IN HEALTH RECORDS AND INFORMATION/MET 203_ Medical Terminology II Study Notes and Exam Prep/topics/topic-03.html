ü¶Ä Describe types of cancer and treatment

A tumour or neoplasm is a mass of tissue that grows faster than normal in an uncoordinated manner.

Treatment of cancers is by radiation therapy and chemotherapy

Tumours are classified as benign or malignant.

üü¢ Benign tumours

  • Have slow growth
  • Cells resemble tissue of origin
  • They are encapsulated
  • Does not metastise

üî¥ Malignant tumours

  • Rapid growth
  • Cells poorly differentiated
  • Not encapsulated
  • Spreads fast via lymph, blood, body cavity

-trophy, -plasia, and -genesis are all suffixes meaning development and growth of tissues in the body.:
