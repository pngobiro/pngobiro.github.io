Health Statistics Overview and Key Concepts

Health Records and Information Technology (Kenya Medical Training College)

Course Units

Select a topic to begin studying:

  • 1 Introduction and Basic Concepts
    Definitions of Health Statistics, Descriptive vs. Inferential Statistics, Variables (Qualitative/Quantitative), and Independent/Dependent Variables.
  • 2 Data Collection Methods
    Primary vs. Secondary Data, Observation, Interviews, Questionnaires (Design, Types, Advantages/Disadvantages).
  • 3 Sampling Methods
    Population vs. Sample, Random Sampling, Systematic Sampling, Stratified Sampling, Cluster Sampling, and Sample Size Determination.
  • 4 Data Classification and Presentation
    Tabulation, Frequency Distributions, Bar Charts, Pie Charts, Histograms, Time Series (Z-Charts), and Lorenz Curves.
  • 5 Health Administrative Statistics & Evaluation
    In-patient Statistics (Bed Occupancy, ALOS, Turnover), Evaluation Techniques, and Maternal Child Health Indicators.
  • 6 Measures of Central Tendency & Dispersion
    Mean, Median, Mode, Geometric/Harmonic Mean, Range, Quartiles, Mean Deviation, and Standard Deviation.
