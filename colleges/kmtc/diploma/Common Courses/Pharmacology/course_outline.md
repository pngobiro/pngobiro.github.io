# Course Outline: Pharmacology

## Course Overview
This course covers the study of drug action on biological systems.

## Modules
- Pharmacology I (PHR 2112)
- Pharmacology II (PHR 2212)
- Pharmacology III

## Key Topics
- General Pharmacology (Pharmacokinetics, Pharmacodynamics)
- Autonomic Nervous System Pharmacology
- Central Nervous System Pharmacology
- Cardiovascular Pharmacology
- Chemotherapy of Infectious Diseases
- Endocrine Pharmacology
- Toxicology
