About These Papers

This collection contains Final Qualifying Examinations (FQE) for the Community Health Nursing diploma program. These papers cover essential topics including community diagnosis, epidemiology, communicable diseases, primary health care (PHC), and maternal/child health.

Key Topics Covered

  • Community Diagnosis
  • Immunization (KEPI)
  • Communicable Diseases
  • Family Planning & MCH
  • Environmental Health
  • Health Administration
