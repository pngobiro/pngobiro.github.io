üìñ PREFACE

This manual is compiled to provide detailed information on the essential tools and technologies designed to support the miraculous journey of pregnancy and childbirth.

The field of maternal healthcare has seen remarkable advancements over the years, and with these developments come the need for clear guidance on the operation, maintenance, and benefits of the specialized equipment that plays a crucial role in ensuring the safety and comfort of both mother and child.

From ultrasounds that offer us the first glimpse of new life to the sophisticated monitors that track vital signs during labor and delivery, each piece of machinery is a testament to the intersection of human care and technological innovation. This manual aims not only to educate but also to instill confidence in clinical engineering students as they navigate the operation of various maternity equipment and their preventative maintenance.

As you turn these pages, I hope you gain valuable insights into the functionalities that make each machine an integral part of maternity wards worldwide. The information within this manual has been compiled from teaching notes of various clinical engineering lectures from Kenya Medical Training College. Remember that these notes are free copyright notes and any lecturer from Kenya Medical Training College is free to use, and I'd greatly appreciate if somebody was to add more information to this manual for the benefits of the students.

üìã MODULE CONTENT:

Theory:

Introduction to maternity Equipment; types, functions. Layout of maternity department; explanation of maternity department layout, description of the activities in the maternity layout.

Revision of reproductive system; explanation of anatomy and physiology of the reproductive system, description of the process of childbirth, explanation of the abnormalities in the reproductive system, listing of the various equipment in maternities, statement of the hygiene requirement in maternity. Weighing scales; explanation of the principles of operation of different types of baby weighing scales, explanation of the function of main parts of weighing scales, description of the appropriate care and maintenance on weighing scales, listing typical faults in weighing scales, explanation of the appropriate repair techniques of typical faults, description of the calibration procedures on baby weighing scales. Vacuum pumps; description of the function and operation of different suction pumps and their respective parts, explanation of the inspection procedures, explanation of the typical faults, explanation of the repair techniques.

Vacuum extractor; explanation of the principles of operation of vacuum extractors, explanation of the function of main parts of vacuum extractors, description of the appropriate care and maintenance, listing typical faults, explanation of the appropriate repair techniques of typical faults, description of the calibration and safety test on vacuum extractors. Infant incubators; explanation of the medical need of infant incubators, explanation of the principle of operation of different types of infant incubators, explanation of the function of main parts, description of the appropriate care and maintenance, listing typical faults, explanation of the appropriate repair techniques of typical faults, description of the calibration and safety tests on infant incubators.

Foetal monitor; parts, use, operation and maintenance. Baby warmer; parts, use, operation and maintenance. Nebulizer; parts, use, operation and maintenance. Photo-box; parts, use, operation and maintenance.
