04

üìê Drafting Instruments

Set-squares

Normally two in number:

  • $30^{\circ}$ - $60^{\circ}$ set-square
  • $45^{\circ}$ set-square

Used to draw lines inclined with the horizontal. With the help of two set-square and a T-square angle of $15^{\circ}$, $30^{\circ}$, $45^{\circ}$, $60^{\circ}$, $75^{\circ}$, $90^{\circ}$, $105^{\circ}$, etc with the horizontal may be drawn. Circle can be divided into different parts.

Protractor

  • Used for measuring and laying off angle.
  • It is usually semi-circular in shape and made of either metal or transparent plastic.
  • Line joining $0^{\circ}$-$180^{\circ}$ is called the base of the protractor.

Mini drafter

Functionality

  • Mini drafter serves the purpose of T-square, set-square, protractor and scale.
  • Two blade are accurately set at right angle to each other.
