üî≥ Types of Bolts

There are distinct types of bolts used in engineering, classified mainly by their head shape.

Hexagon Bolt and Nut

The most common type of bolt with a six-sided head.

Square Bolt and Nut

A bolt with a four-sided head, often used in heavy machinery.

üõ†Ô∏è Bolt Application

Bolts are used to connect two pieces that are not too thick. Two clearance holes are drilled on both pieces in order to pass through the bolt.

Assembly Sequence

Step 1

Insert a bolt into a clearance hole.

Step 2

Insert a washer.

Step 3

Screw a nut.
