üí∞ Compensation & Rewards

Compensation-Covers all rewards that individuals receive as a result of their employment and consequent expenditure of their efforts, time and energy in the organization.

It consists of:

  1. a)Pay/base/basic pay - the money that a person receives for performing a job e.g. salary/wages.
  2. b)Compensation-Covers all rewards that individuals receive as a result of their employment and consequent expenditure of their efforts, time and energy in the organization. It consists of:
  3. c)a) Pay/base/basic pay - the money that a person receives for performing a job e.g. salary/wages.
  4. d)) Incentives- compensation given to employees for extra tasks, better performance or unique contribution, e.g. over time pay, commissions.
  5. e)-These will inspire the staff to take up extra responsibilities, improve performance and have job satisfaction.

It encompasses health and safety.

Health - employees freedom from illness; mental, physical, spiritual and their general wellbeing.
