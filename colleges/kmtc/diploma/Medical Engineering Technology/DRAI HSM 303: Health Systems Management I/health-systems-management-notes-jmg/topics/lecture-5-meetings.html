ü§ù Skills for Managing Meetings

  1. 1.Plan well by deciding the purpose of the meeting.
  2. 2.Conduct them well with a set of ground rules understood by all.
  3. 3.Follow to ensure that assignments are completed.

üìÖ When to hold meetings.

  • ‚Ä¢When conveying the same message to everyone at once and getting feedback.
  • ‚Ä¢When seeking new ideas.
  • ‚Ä¢When identifying and solving other problems.
  • ‚Ä¢When gaining support or commitment.
  • ‚Ä¢Bringing a variety of specialized perspectives to bear on a problem.

üõãÔ∏è Meeting arrangements.

The arrangements should be made in advance.

  1. 1.Decide who is to attend the meeting according to the purpose of the meeting, i.e., individuals with decision-making authority may be important for the meeting.
  2. 2.Arrange for physical comfort, e.g., room, seating arrangements, writing materials, food, parking space, security, etc.
  3. 3.Develop and distribute the agenda.

üìù Planning an agenda

The agenda should:

  1. a)Itemize the points of business.
  2. b)Arrange them in order according to priorities and time needed.
  3. c)Notify those who will be responsible for presenting information.

Importance of Agenda.

  1. 1.Allows members to know what to expect.
  2. 2.Enables members to prepare.
  3. 3.Provides order for dealing with issues.
  4. 4.Facilitates responsibility for members to attend.
  5. 5.Allows responsibilities to be delegated.

Agenda format

  1. 1.Formal or standing agenda. It remains the same from meeting to meeting.
  2. 2.Informal flexible agenda. Subject to change and permits shifts in the order of business.
  3. 3.Timed agenda. Apportions the amount of time and establishes the sequence of topics to be covered.
  4. 4.Discipline agenda. Explains something about a topic or decision to be made.

‚úÖ Writing a successful to-do list.

  1. 1.Write it daily.
  2. 2.Be realistic and aware of limits of time available.
  3. 3.Do not over-schedule.
  4. 4.Allow time cushion in between.
  5. 5.Review the list every morning.
  6. 6.Before doing each item, ask why me (delegate when possible).
  7. 7.Group related activities together.
