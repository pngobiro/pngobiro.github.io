üì§ Basic Organization

Division Characteristics:

  • Information carried away from CNS to effector organs
  • Two main divisions:
    • Autonomic nervous system (involuntary)
    • Somatic nervous system (voluntary)
  • Primary neurotransmitters:
    • Acetylcholine (ACh)
    • Norepinephrine (NE)

‚öñÔ∏è System Comparison

Autonomic Nervous System:

  • Involuntary control
  • Innervates:
    • Cardiac muscle
    • Smooth muscle
    • Glands
  • Two-neuron chain:
    • Preganglionic neuron
    • Postganglionic neuron

Somatic Nervous System:

  • Voluntary control
  • Innervates:
    • Skeletal muscle
  • Single neuron:
    • Motor neuron directly innervates muscle
    • Always releases ACh
    • Always excitatory
