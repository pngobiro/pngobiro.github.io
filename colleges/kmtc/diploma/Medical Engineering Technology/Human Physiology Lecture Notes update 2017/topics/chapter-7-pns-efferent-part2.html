Chapter 7: PNS Efferent Division (continued)

üîÑ Autonomic Nervous System Organization

Two-Neuron Chain:

  • Preganglionic fiber:
    • Cell body in CNS
    • Synapses with postganglionic fiber in ganglion
  • Postganglionic fiber:
    • Innervates the effector organ

Divisions:

  1. Sympathetic Division
    • Preganglionic fibers from thoracic and lumbar spinal cord
    • Short preganglionic fibers synapse in sympathetic chain ganglion
    • Some pass through to collateral ganglion near organ
    • Long postganglionic fibers release NE
  2. Parasympathetic Division
    • Preganglionic fibers from brain stem or sacral cord
    • Long preganglionic fibers to terminal ganglia
    • Short postganglionic fibers release ACh

üß™ Neurotransmitter Systems

  • Cholinergic fibers release ACh:
    • All preganglionic fibers (sympathetic and parasympathetic)
    • All parasympathetic postganglionic fibers
    • Some sympathetic postganglionic (sweat glands)
  • Adrenergic fibers release NE:
    • Most sympathetic postganglionic fibers
  • Postganglionic terminals have varicosities:
    • Release neurotransmitter over large area
    • Influence whole organs
