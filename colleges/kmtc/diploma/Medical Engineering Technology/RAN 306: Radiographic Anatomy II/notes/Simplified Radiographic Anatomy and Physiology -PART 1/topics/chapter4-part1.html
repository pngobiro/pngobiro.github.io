ü¶¥ Introduction to the Skeletal System

The skeletal system is the strong internal framework of the human body around which other body structures are built.

Components of Skeletal System

The skeletal system is mainly composed of:

  • Bones
  • Cartilage
  • Joints
  • Tendons
  • Ligaments

Functions of the Skeletal System

  • Support of the body
  • Facilitation of body movement
  • Protection of internal organs
  • Production of red blood cells
  • Storage and release of minerals and fats

Main Divisions

The Skeletal System is mainly subdivided into two main parts:

  1. The axial skeleton
  2. The appendicular skeleton
