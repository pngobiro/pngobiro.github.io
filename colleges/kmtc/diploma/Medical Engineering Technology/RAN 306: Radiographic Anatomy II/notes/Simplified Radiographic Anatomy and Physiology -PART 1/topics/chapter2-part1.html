üßç Anatomical Position

For precision, there is standard way to view the human body anatomically. It is that of the human body standing upright position and facing forward, with straight, parallel feet and at shoulder width with toes directed forward, the upper limbs are held out to each side, and the palms facing forward with extended fingers and thumb points anteriorly.
