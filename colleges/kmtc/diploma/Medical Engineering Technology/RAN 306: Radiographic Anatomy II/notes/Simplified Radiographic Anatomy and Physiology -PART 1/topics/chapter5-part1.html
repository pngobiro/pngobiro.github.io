The Skull

The Skull is the skeletal framework of the head; it is the bony structure that forms the protective cavity for the brain and supports the face. It is comprised of many bones which are joined by fibrous joints known as sutures.

üîç Skull Divisions

The skull can be divided into two divisions:

  • ‚Üí Cranium: Also known as the skullcap, it is the upper part of the skull represents the brain case, it surrounds the brain and protects its components, and it mainly houses the meninges, cerebral vasculature and the structures of the middle and inner ear.
  • ‚Üí Facial bones: They underlie the facial structures and supports the soft tissues of the face, enclose the eyeballs, form the nasal and oral cavities, and support the teeth of the upper and lower jaws.
