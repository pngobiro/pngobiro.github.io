❓ Q&A Section 13

Film Processing Chemistry and Medical Q&A

Finished radiographs should retain what % moisture?

  • B.10%-15% ✅

Staining or fading results when too much ______ remains on film:

  • C.thiosulfate ✅

Fixing agent used to clear undeveloped silver halide:

  • D.ammonium thiosulfate ✅

pH environment for proper development:

  • A.alkaline ✅

Solutions responsible for reducing exposed silver halide:

  • C.hydroquinone and phenidone ✅

Sequential order for processing film:

  • C.developing, fixing, washing, dryer ✅

Conversion of latent to manifest image is accomplished by:

  • B.Radiographic processing ✅

Diagnosis of idiopathic pulmonary haemosiderosis (IPH) is confirmed by:

Bronchoalveolar lavage (BAL) ✅

Causes of postpartum hemorrhage include all except:

Massaging the fundus of uterus after placental delivery ✅

Sarcoidosis affects kidneys most commonly by:

Nephrocalcinosis secondary to overproduction of calcitriol from extrarenal granulomata ✅

Presentation of vascular Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome:

Vascular hemorrhages and gastrointestinal bleeds ✅

Histological hallmark of sarcoidosis:

Non-caseating epithelioid cell granulomas ✅

Processes by which a plica becomes symptomatic:

Idiopathic, Trauma, Inflammation, Rupture ✅

Abandoned VP shunt migration and bowel perforation:

Can be a chronic erosive process which develops slowly through local erosion ✅

CTA findings of coronary sinus communication to left atrium:

A bridging vein connecting the coronary sinus to the left atrium ✅
