Technique Charts, Digital Imaging, and Processing

Condition required for technique charts to be effective:

  • A.Equipment must be calibrated to perform properly ✅

Which factor would NOT be standardized on technique charts:

  • D.Patient age ✅

Technique system using fixed mAs regardless of part thickness:

  • C.Manual ✅

Most important when using a technique chart:

  • C.The part is measured accurately ✅

Advantage of fixed kVp technique chart:

  • B.It reduces patient exposure ✅

Disadvantage of variable kVp technique chart:

  • D.It increases heat load on the x-ray tube ✅

Most important in creating any exposure technique chart:

  • A.Achieving adequate penetration of the anatomic part ✅

The "halo artifact" is associated with:

  • c)Edge enhancement ✅

A bit depth of one (1) results in how many colors?

  • c)2 ✅

In a CRT video display, the speed images are drawn is called:

  • a)Refresh rate ✅

Modality work list is compiled from:

  • a)HIS and RIS ✅

"Metal mottle" is associated with:

  • b)Equalization ✅

Proper way to correct image appearance when default menu fails:

  • b)Redefine the menu's processing codes ✅

Personnel with deep software privileges:

  • b)Super users ✅

Delayed light emission after refresh is called:

  • d)Persistence ✅

In digital radiography, image brightness is adjusted by:

  • d)Window level ✅

In digital radiography, contrast is adjusted by:

  • d)Window width ✅

To achieve better spatial resolution, dot pitch must:

  • c)Increase ✅

ACR minimum luminance standard:

  • d)160 candelas/m² ✅

Ratio of screen width to height:

  • d)Aspect ratio ✅

Altering which image characteristics may cause permanent data loss?

  • b)Brightness, contrast, ROI location (all) ✅

Difference between max and min luminance is:

  • b)Contrast resolution ✅

Common plus-density artifact from bending film:

  • A.Half-moon marks ✅

Silver recovery unit with electrically charged drum:

  • B.Electrolytic ✅

Safelight filters are chosen based on:

  • C.Film sensitivity ✅

Proper film storage conditions:

  • D.50-70°F, 40-60% humidity ✅

Decreasing developer temperature:

  • C.Decreases density ✅

Processing chemicals are replenished primarily due to:

  • A.Exhaustion ✅

Roller moving film from one tank to another:

  • D.Crossover ✅

Roller moving film from bottom of tank upward:

  • C.Turnaround ✅
