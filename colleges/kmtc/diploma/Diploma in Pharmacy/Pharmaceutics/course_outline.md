# Course Outline: Pharmaceutics

## Course Overview
This course covers the science of dosage form design and drug delivery systems.

## Modules
- Pharmaceutics I
- Pharmaceutics II
- Pharmaceutics III
- Pharmaceutics IV
- Pharmaceutics V

## Key Topics
- Introduction to Pharmaceutics
- Pharmaceutical Calculations
- Dosage Forms (Tablets, Capsules, Liquids, Ointments, etc.)
- Drug Stability
- Biopharmaceutics
- Novel Drug Delivery Systems
