# Course Outline: Clinical Pharmacy

## Course Overview
This course focuses on the application of pharmaceutical knowledge to patient care, ensuring the safe and effective use of medications.

## Modules
- Clinical Pharmacy I
- Clinical Pharmacy II
- Clinical Pharmacy III

## Key Topics
- Introduction to Clinical Pharmacy
- Pharmaceutical Care
- Medication Therapy Management
- Drug Interactions and Adverse Drug Reactions
- Patient Counseling and Education
- Pharmacotherapy of common diseases
