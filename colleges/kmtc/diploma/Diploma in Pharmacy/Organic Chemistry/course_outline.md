# Course Outline: Organic Chemistry

## Course Overview
This course covers the structure, properties, and reactions of organic compounds.

## Modules
- Organic Chemistry I
- Organic Chemistry II

## Key Topics
- Structure and Bonding in Organic Molecules
- Functional Groups (Alkanes, Alkenes, Alcohols, etc.)
- Stereochemistry
- Organic Reaction Mechanisms
- Heterocyclic Chemistry
