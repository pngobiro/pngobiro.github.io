# Course Outline: Inorganic Pharmaceutical Chemistry

## Course Overview
This course focuses on inorganic substances used in pharmacy and medicine.

## Modules
- Inorganic Pharmaceutical Chemistry I
- Inorganic Pharmaceutical Chemistry II

## Key Topics
- Pharmaceutical aids and necessities
- Gastrointestinal agents (antacids, adsorbents)
- Topical agents (antimicrobials, astringents)
- Dental products
- Major intra- and extra-cellular electrolytes
- Radiopharmaceuticals
