# Course Outline: Pharmaceutical Dispensing Practice

## Course Overview
This course covers the practical aspects of dispensing medications to patients.

## Modules
- Pharmaceutical Dispensing Practice I
- Pharmaceutical Dispensing Practice II
- Pharmaceutical Dispensing Practice III

## Key Topics
- Prescription Handling and Verification
- Dosage Forms and Calculations
- Labeling and Packaging
- Compounding of Extemporaneous Preparations
- Patient Counseling
- Dispensing Errors and Safety
