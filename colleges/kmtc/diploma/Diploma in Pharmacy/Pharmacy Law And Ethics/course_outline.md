# Course Outline: Pharmacy Law And Ethics

## Course Overview
This course covers the legal and ethical framework governing pharmacy practice in Kenya.

## Modules
- Pharmacy Law and Ethics I
- Pharmacy Law and Ethics II

## Key Topics
- Pharmacy and Poisons Act (Cap 244)
- Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act
- Food, Drugs and Chemical Substances Act
- Code of Ethics for Pharmacists and Pharmaceutical Technologists
- Regulation of Pharmacy Practice
