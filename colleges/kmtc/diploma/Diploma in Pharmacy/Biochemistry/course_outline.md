# Course Outline: Biochemistry

## Course Overview
This course covers the chemical processes and substances that occur within living organisms. It provides a foundation for understanding drug metabolism and mechanism of action.

## Modules
- Biochemistry I
- Biochemistry II

## Key Topics
- Structure and function of biomolecules (proteins, carbohydrates, lipids, nucleic acids)
- Enzymes and enzyme kinetics
- Metabolism (glycolysis, Krebs cycle, oxidative phosphorylation)
- Molecular biology basics
