# Course Outline: Medical Microbiology

## Course Overview
This course covers microorganisms that cause disease and the principles of immunology.

## Modules
- Medical Microbiology I
- Medical Microbiology II

## Key Topics
- Introduction to Microbiology
- Bacteriology (structure, classification, pathogenic bacteria)
- Virology
- Mycology
- Parasitology
- Immunology and Serology
- Sterilization and Disinfection
