# Course Outline: Medicinal Chemistry

## Course Overview
This course deals with the design, development, and synthesis of pharmaceutical drugs.

## Modules
- Medicinal Chemistry I
- Medicinal Chemistry II
- Medicinal Chemistry III

## Key Topics
- Drug Design and Development
- Structure-Activity Relationships (SAR)
- Physicochemical properties of drugs
- Chemistry of major drug classes (Antibiotics, Analgesics, etc.)
- Drug Metabolism
