# Course Outline: Pharmacognosy

## Course Overview
This course covers the study of medicines derived from natural sources.

## Modules
- Pharmacognosy I (PCG 306)
- Pharmacognosy II (PCG 406)

## Key Topics
- Definition, history, and scope of Pharmacognosy
- Classification of crude drugs (Alphabetical, Taxonomical, etc.)
- Quality control and evaluation of crude drugs
- Cultivation, collection, and processing of medicinal plants
- Study of major groups: Alkaloids, Glycosides, Volatile Oils, Tannins, Resins
- Traditional Systems of Medicine
- Nutraceuticals and Herbal Cosmetics
