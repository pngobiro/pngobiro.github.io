Immunizations Course

Duration: 4 hours Level: Beginner

Course Overview

This course provides an in-depth understanding of immunizations, including key definitions, various types of vaccines, national immunization schedules, and detailed information on immunizable diseases. The content is designed for healthcare professionals, students, and anyone interested in learning about immunizations and their importance in public health.

🎯 Course Objectives

  • Understand the basic concepts of immunity and immunization.
  • Describe different types of vaccines and their mechanisms of action.
  • Become familiar with the national immunization schedule.
  • Identify and describe common immunizable diseases and their prevention.

Course Modules

  1. Introduction to Immunization

    This module covers the definitions of immunization, immunity, and types of immunity.

    60 minutes

  2. Vaccines

    This module explores different types of vaccines and their characteristics.

    60 minutes

  3. National Immunisation Schedule

    This module focuses on the national immunization schedule and specific vaccines.

    60 minutes

  4. Immunizable Diseases and Reactions

    This module details immunizable diseases, their symptoms, and management strategies.

    60 minutes

Prerequisites

  • Basic understanding of biology and health sciences.
