Schering bridge is used to measure the value of capacitance. The circuit diagram of Schering bridge is shown in the below figure.

Let, $Z_{1}, Z_{2}, Z_{3}$ and $Z 4$ are the impedances of arms $D A, A B \geqslant D$ and $B C$ respectively. The values of these impedances will be

$$\begin{aligned} Z_{1} &= \frac{R_{1}\left(\frac{1}{j \omega C_{1}}\right)}{R_{1}+\frac{1}{j \omega C_{1}}}\\ \Rightarrow Z_{1} &= \frac{R_{1}}{1+j \omega R_{1} C_{1}}\\ Z_{2} &= R_{2}\\ Z_{3} &= \frac{1}{j \omega C_{3}}\\ Z_{4} &= R_{4}+\frac{1}{j \omega C_{4}}\\ \Rightarrow Z_{4} &= \frac{1+j \omega R_{4} C_{4}}{j \omega C_{4}} \end{aligned}$$

Substitute these impedance values in the following balancing condition of AC bridge.

$$\begin{aligned} \frac{1+j \omega R_{4} C_{4}}{j \omega C_{4}}&=\frac{R_{2}\left(\frac{1}{j \omega C_{3}}\right)}{\frac{R_{1}}{1+j \omega R_{1} C_{1}}}\\ \Rightarrow \frac{1+j \omega R_{4} C_{4}}{j \omega C_{4}}&=\frac{R_{2}\left(1+j \omega R_{1} C_{1}\right)}{j \omega R_{1} C_{3}}\\ \Rightarrow \frac{1+j \omega R_{4} C_{4}}{C_{4}}&=\frac{R_{2}\left(1+j \omega R_{1} C_{1}\right)}{R_{1} C_{3}}\\ \Rightarrow \frac{1}{C_{4}}+j \omega R_{4}&=\frac{R_{2}}{R_{1} C_{3}}+\frac{j \omega C_{1} R_{2}}{C_{3}} \end{aligned}$$

By comparing the respective real and imaginary terms of above equation, we will get

$$\begin{aligned} C_{4}&=\frac{R_{1} C_{3}}{R_{2}} \quad \text { Equation 1 }\\ R_{4}&=\frac{C_{1} R_{2}}{C_{3}} \quad \text { Equation 2 } \end{aligned}$$
