2. A system consists of 5 components in series each having a reliability of 0.97 . What is the reliability of the system?

Rel $=0.97^{5}=0.86$
With series systems, reliability decreases as the number of components increases.
With 6 components in series;
Rel $=0.97^{6}=0.832972$
With 7 components; Rel $=0.97^{7}=0.8079828$
