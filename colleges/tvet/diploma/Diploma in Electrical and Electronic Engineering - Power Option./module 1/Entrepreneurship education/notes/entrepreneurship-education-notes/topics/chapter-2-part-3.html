Economic, Social and Political Factors

Factors Affecting Entrepreneurial Development

  1. High Taxation Levels

    • For business and personal incomes which reduce profits earned making it unattractive to engage in business
    • Taxation of raw materials and other inputs raise production costs

  2. Corruption and Official Harassment

    • Entrepreneurs are forced to bribe officials in various government departments to allow operation or start up
    • Raids under one pretext or another which tends to be very harassing

  3. Unregulated Competition

    • From the outside world due to liberalization
    • Opened importation competing with locally produced goods

  4. Declining Personal Incomes

    • Due to over-increasing cost of living
    • Rise in unemployment

  5. High Cost of Finance

    • The cost of borrowing is high
    • Business collapses because they lack ability to repay loans

  6. Lack of Necessary Skills and Knowledge

    • Due to lack of training opportunities
    • High education costs

  7. Poor Infrastructure

    • Poor transport and communication network
    • Making business difficult
    • Inconveniencing consumers
    • High energy costs

  8. Cultural Barriers

    • Lack of entrepreneurial culture
    • Traditional beliefs and practices that may hinder business development
