Role of an Entrepreneur in an Enterprise

1. Risk and Uncertainty Management

The bearing of uncertainty is the primary function of the entrepreneur i.e. losses or profits.

2. Business Management

The management of the business enterprise can be delegated but ultimate responsibility lies with the entrepreneur.

3. Capital and Innovation

Provision of risk capital and invention.

4. Opportunity Identification

Identifying gaps in the market and turning such gaps to business opportunities i.e. to initiate a business.

5. Resource Management

Financing the businesses, through raising and mobilizing the necessary resources to exploit opportunity.

6. Environmental Scanning

Searching for business opportunities through environmental scans.

7. Resource Mobilization

Mobilization of resources needed to start and run a business from various sources:

  • Personal savings
  • Friends & relatives
  • Financial institutions etc.

8. Opportunity Evaluation

Evaluation of business opportunities to access viability and any other benefits that might accrue to the business.

9. Leadership

Provide the necessary leadership for the business and those working in it.
