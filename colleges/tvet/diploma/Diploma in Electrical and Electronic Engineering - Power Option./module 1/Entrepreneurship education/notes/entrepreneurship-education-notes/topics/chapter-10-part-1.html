Emerging Trends: Technological Aspects

Understanding Technology in Business

Technology encompasses everything that enables us to perform activities, including knowledge, skills, tools, equipment, machinery, and production processes. Business operations such as production, marketing, research and development, and communications are significantly affected by the level and type of technology available in the business environment.

Impact on Business Operations

  • Production processes and efficiency
  • Marketing strategies and reach
  • Research and development capabilities
  • Communication methods and effectiveness
  • Overall business competitiveness

Key Technological Considerations

1. Cost of Acquiring New Technology

New technologies that are emerging will be costly compared to outdated technologies. Entrepreneurs must achieve a balance between:

  • Stage of the technology
  • Capital cost requirements
  • Expected returns on investment
  • Business scale and needs

2. Standardization

The selection of technology by the entrepreneur should help in standardizing the end products for:

  • Market acceptance
  • Industry uniformity
  • Quality consistency
  • Process efficiency

3. Technical Support and Maintenance

The selection of new technology should be supported by:

  • Proper maintenance services
  • Regular technical support
  • Equipment upkeep procedures
  • Productivity maintenance measures

Important Consideration

When evaluating new technology, entrepreneurs must consider the total cost of ownership, including not just the purchase price, but also installation, training, maintenance, and upgrade costs.
