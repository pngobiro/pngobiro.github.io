ICT Tools and E-Business Applications

ICT Tools for Business Efficiency

Phone

Enables direct communication with customers, suppliers, and business partners. Essential for customer service and business relationships.

Fax Machine

Allows transmission of documents and contracts quickly across distances, particularly useful for legal and formal business communications.

World Wide Web

Provides global reach for businesses, enabling online presence, e-commerce, and access to information and resources worldwide.

Radio

Effective medium for advertising and reaching local markets, particularly useful for brand awareness and promotional campaigns.

Television

Powerful visual medium for marketing and brand building, reaching wide audiences with audio-visual content.

Computer

Essential tool for business operations, data management, accounting, and various business processes automation.

E-mail

Facilitates quick, efficient, and documented communication with stakeholders, essential for modern business correspondence.

E-Business Concepts

E-government

The use of digital technologies to provide government services to citizens and businesses. Benefits include:

  • Improved service delivery
  • Increased transparency
  • Reduced bureaucracy
  • 24/7 access to services
  • Cost reduction in service provision

E-procurement

The electronic purchase and sale of supplies, services, and works. Features include:

  • Online tender processes
  • Digital supplier management
  • Automated purchase orders
  • Electronic invoicing
  • Supply chain integration

E-business

The conduct of business on the internet. Key aspects include:

  • Online marketing and sales
  • Digital customer service
  • Electronic payment systems
  • Supply chain management
  • Virtual collaboration
