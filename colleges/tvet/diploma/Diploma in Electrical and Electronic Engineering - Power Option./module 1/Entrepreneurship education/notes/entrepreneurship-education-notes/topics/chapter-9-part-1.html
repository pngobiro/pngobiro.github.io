ICT in Entrepreneurship

Key Concepts

Information

A product of data which has been given a structure and put into a context.

Communication

The art of sending and receiving messages or information from one person to another via a channel.

Technology

The generation of knowledge and processes to develop systems that solve problems and extend human capabilities.

Benefits of ICT to Small Business

  • Improved accuracy, internally and externally
  • More comprehensive services to customers
  • Faster processing and prompter responses to customers
  • Better information for management previously unavailable
  • New customer services previously not possible
  • New sources of information for improved product design and marketing
  • Reduced costs through greater staff productivity
  • More attractive, cleaner working environment helping recruitment and retention

How ICT Helps Small Business Enterprise

1. Information Processing Tasks

Tasks range from:

  • Computing and printing payroll checks
  • Creating presentations
  • Setting up websites for customer orders

2. Decision Making Tasks

Involves the use of online analytical processing to manipulate information, including:

  • Performing simple database queries
  • Using sophisticated artificial intelligence tools
  • Employing neural networks and genetic algorithms

3. Shared Information through Decentralized Computing

Creates an environment where:

  • Enterprise splits computing power across locations
  • Information is organized centrally but accessible anywhere
  • Employees can access resources from different devices

4. Innovation

Enables creation of:

  • New devices (e.g., cellular phones with computer technology)
  • New processes (e.g., satellite navigation)
  • New ideas for business improvement
