SECTION A: ICT (40 marks)

Answer any TWO questions from this section.

Question 1

(a) Define the term "computer" and state three main functions of a computer.

(b) Outline four differences between Random Access Memory (RAM) and Read Only Memory (ROM).

(c) State three advantages of command line interface over graphical user interface.

(d) Define the term networking protocol and state two examples.

Answer

(a) Computer definition and functions:

A computer is an electronic device that accepts input data, processes it according to predefined instructions, and produces output.

Three main functions:

  • Data input and storage
  • Data processing
  • Information output

(b) Differences between RAM and ROM:

  • Volatility: RAM loses data when power is off; ROM retains data
  • Modification: RAM can be written to and modified; ROM is permanent
  • Usage: RAM used for temporary program execution; ROM for system startup
  • Speed: RAM is faster than ROM

(c) Advantages of command line interface:

  • Uses less system resources than GUI
  • Faster execution of commands
  • More precise control over system operations

(d) Networking protocol:

A set of rules that govern how data is transmitted between devices in a network.

Examples:

  • TCP/IP (Transmission Control Protocol/Internet Protocol)
  • HTTP (Hypertext Transfer Protocol)

Question 2

(a) State three characteristics of a computer virus.

(b) List four advantages of using electronic spreadsheets over manual spreadsheets.

(c) Explain three factors to consider when choosing a printer.

(d) Outline two advantages and two disadvantages of using optical storage devices.

Answer

(a) Characteristics of a computer virus:

  • Self-replication capability
  • Ability to attach to host files
  • Causes damage to computer systems

(b) Advantages of electronic spreadsheets:

  • Automatic calculation and recalculation
  • Easy data modification and updating
  • Multiple formatting options
  • Advanced data analysis capabilities

(c) Factors to consider when choosing a printer:

  • Print quality and resolution
  • Printing speed and volume capacity
  • Cost of printer and consumables

(d) Optical storage devices:

Advantages:

  • High storage capacity
  • Durable and portable

Disadvantages:

  • Relatively slow access speed
  • Susceptible to physical damage

Question 3

(a) Explain three ways of protecting data in a computer system.

(b) State four functions of an operating system.

(c) List three examples of system software.

(d) Define the term "computer network" and state two types of computer networks.

Answer

(a) Ways of protecting data:

  • Using passwords and encryption
  • Regular data backup
  • Installing antivirus software

(b) Functions of an operating system:

  • Memory management
  • Process management
  • File management
  • Device management

(c) Examples of system software:

  • Operating systems (e.g., Windows, Linux)
  • Device drivers
  • Utility programs

(d) Computer network:

A collection of computers and devices connected together to share resources and information.

Types:

  • Local Area Network (LAN)
  • Wide Area Network (WAN)

SECTION B: COMMUNICATION SKILLS (20 marks)

Answer any ONE question from this section.

Question 4

(a) Explain four barriers to effective communication.

(b) State three advantages of written communication.

(c) List three elements of non-verbal communication.

(d) Write a memorandum to all staff members informing them about the upcoming annual general meeting.

Answer

(a) Barriers to effective communication:

  • Language barriers - differences in language or technical jargon
  • Physical barriers - noise, distance, poor connectivity
  • Psychological barriers - emotions, prejudices, assumptions
  • Cultural barriers - different cultural interpretations and norms

(b) Advantages of written communication:

  • Provides permanent record for future reference
  • Allows for careful composition and revision
  • Can reach multiple recipients simultaneously

(c) Elements of non-verbal communication:

  • Body language and gestures
  • Facial expressions
  • Eye contact

(d) Memorandum:

INTERNAL MEMORANDUM

TO: All Staff Members
FROM: Management
DATE: [Current Date]
SUBJECT: Annual General Meeting

This is to inform all staff members that the Annual General Meeting will be held as follows:

Date: [Date]
Time: 9:00 AM
Venue: Main Conference Room

All staff members are required to attend. Please ensure you are seated by 8:45 AM.

Agenda will be circulated prior to the meeting.

[Signature]
Management

Question 5

(a) List four characteristics of effective feedback.

(b) State three purposes of communication in an organization.

(c) Outline three ways of improving listening skills.

(d) Write a letter of application in response to an advertised position of ICT Officer.

Answer

(a) Characteristics of effective feedback:

  • Specific and clear
  • Timely and relevant
  • Constructive and objective
  • Action-oriented

(b) Purposes of communication in an organization:

  • Information sharing and decision making
  • Coordination of activities
  • Motivation and employee engagement

(c) Ways of improving listening skills:

  • Maintaining eye contact
  • Avoiding interruptions
  • Taking notes when necessary

(d) Application Letter:

SECTION C: ENTREPRENEURSHIP (40 marks)

Answer any TWO questions from this section.

Question 6

(a) Define entrepreneurship and state three characteristics of an entrepreneur.

(b) Explain four environmental factors that affect business operations.

(c) Outline three advantages of partnership business.

Answer

(a) Entrepreneurship and characteristics:

Entrepreneurship is the process of creating, organizing, and managing a business venture to make a profit while taking risks.

Characteristics of an entrepreneur:

  • Risk-taking ability
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Decision-making skills

(b) Environmental factors affecting business:

  • Political factors - government policies and regulations
  • Economic factors - interest rates, inflation, market conditions
  • Social factors - cultural values, demographics
  • Technological factors - technological advancement and changes

(c) Advantages of partnership:

  • Shared capital and resources
  • Combined expertise and skills
  • Shared responsibilities and risks

Question 7

(a) Explain four sources of business ideas.

(b) State three ways of promoting small-scale businesses.

(c) Outline three roles of profit in business.

Answer

(a) Sources of business ideas:

  • Market gaps and consumer needs
  • Previous work experience
  • Hobbies and interests
  • Research and innovation

(b) Ways of promoting small-scale businesses:

  • Social media marketing
  • Local advertising and word of mouth
  • Promotional events and discounts

(c) Roles of profit in business:

  • Source of business growth and expansion
  • Measure of business performance
  • Motivation for continued operation

Question 8

(a) List four components of a business plan.

(b) State three ways of raising capital for a small business.

(c) Explain three factors to consider when choosing a business location.

Answer

(a) Components of a business plan:

  • Executive summary
  • Market analysis
  • Financial plan
  • Operational plan

(b) Ways of raising capital:

  • Personal savings
  • Bank loans
  • Partnership investment

(c) Factors for choosing business location:

  • Accessibility to target market
  • Cost of rent and utilities
  • Availability of infrastructure
