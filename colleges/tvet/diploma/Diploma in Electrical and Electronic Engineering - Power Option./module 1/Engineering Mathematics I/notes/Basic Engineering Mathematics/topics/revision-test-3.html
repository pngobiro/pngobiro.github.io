Revision Test 3 : Ratio, proportion, powers, roots, indices and units

This assignment covers the material contained in Chapters 6-8. The marks available are shown in brackets at the end of each question.

  1. 1. In a box of 1500 nails, 125 are defective. Express the non-defective nails as a ratio of the defective ones, in its simplest form. (3)
  2. 2. Prize money in a lottery totals ¬£4500 and is shared among three winners in the ratio 5:3:1. How much does the first prize winner receive? (3)
  3. 3. A simple machine has an effort:load ratio of 3:41. Determine the effort, in newtons, to lift a load of 6.15 kN . (4)
  4. 4. If 15 cans of lager weigh 7.8 kg , what will 24 cans weigh? (3)
  5. 5. Hooke's law states that stress is directly proportional to strain within the elastic limit of a material. When for brass the stress is 21 MPa , the strain is $250 \times 10^{-6}$. Determine the stress when the strain is $350 \times 10^{-6}$.(4)
  6. 6. If 12 inches = 30.48 cm, find the number of millimetres in 17 inches. (3)
  7. 7. If $x$ is inversely proportional to $y$ and $x=12$ when $y=0.4$, determine
    (a) the value of $x$ when $y$ is 3 .
    (b) the value of $y$ when $x=2$.(4)
  8. 8. Evaluate
    (a) $3 \times 2^{3} \times 2^{2}$(b) $49^{\frac{1}{2}}$(4)
  9. 9. Evaluate $\frac{3^{2} \times \sqrt{36} \times 2^{2}}{3 \times 81^{\frac{1}{2}}}$ taking positive square roots only. (4)
  10. 10. Evaluate $6^{4} \times 6 \times 6^{2}$ in index form. (2)
  11. 11. Evaluate
    (a) $\frac{2^{7}}{2^{2}}$
    (b) $\frac{10^{4} \times 10 \times 10^{5}}{10^{6} \times 10^{2}}$(4)
  12. 12. Evaluate
    (a) $\frac{2^{3} \times 2 \times 2^{2}}{2^{4}}$
    (b) $\frac{(2^{3} \times 16)^{2}}{(8 \times 2)^{3}}$
    (c) $\left(\frac{1}{4^{2}}\right)^{-1}$(6)
  13. 13. Evaluate
    (a) (27) $-\frac{1}{3}$
    (b) $\frac{(\frac{3}{2})^{-2}-\frac{2}{9}}{(\frac{2}{3})^{2}}$(5)
  14. 14. State the SI unit of (a) capacitance (b) electrical potential (c) work (3)
  15. 15. State the quantity that has an SI unit of (a) kilograms (b) henrys (c) hertz (d) $\mathrm{~m}^{3}$(4)
  16. 16. Express the following in engineering notation in prefix form.
    (a) 250000 J (b) 0.05 H (c) $2 \times 10^{8}$ W (d) $750 \times 10^{-8}$ F (8)
  17. 17. Rewrite (a) 0.0067 mA in $\mu$A (b) $40 \times 10^{4}$ kV as MV(4)
