üìà4.3 DIFFERENTIATION

Let \(y=f(x) \quad (1)\) be a function of x.
Let \(\Delta x\) be a small increment in x and let \(\Delta y\) be the corresponding increment in y.
\(\therefore y+\Delta y=f(x+\Delta x) \quad (2)\)
(2) - (1) \(\quad \Delta y=f(x+\Delta x)-f(x)\)
\(\therefore \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}=\frac{f(x+\Delta x)-f(x)}{\Delta x}\)
Taking the limit as \(\Delta x \rightarrow 0\),
\(\underset{\Delta x \rightarrow 0}{\operatorname{Lt}} \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}=\underset{\Delta x \rightarrow 0}{\operatorname{Lt}} \frac{f(x+\Delta x)-f(x)}{\Delta x}\)
\(\underset{\Delta x \rightarrow 0}{\operatorname{Lt}} \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}\) is called the differential coefficient of y with respect to x and is denoted as \(\frac{d y}{d x}\).
\(\therefore \frac{d y}{d x}=\operatorname{Lt}_{\Delta x \rightarrow 0} \frac{f(x+\Delta x)-f(x)}{\Delta x}\)

List of formulae:

  1. (1) \(\frac{d}{d x}\left(x^{n}\right)=n x^{n-1}\), n a real number
  2. (2) \(\frac{d}{d x}(\sqrt{x})=\frac{1}{2 \sqrt{x}}\)
  3. (3) \(\frac{d}{d x}\left(e^{x}\right)=e^{x}\)
  4. (4) \(\frac{d}{d x}(\log x)=\frac{1}{x}\)
  5. (5) \(\frac{d}{d x}(\sin x)=\cos x\)
  6. (6) \(\frac{d}{d x}(\cos x)=-\sin x\)
  7. (7) \(\frac{d}{d x}(\tan x)=\sec ^{2} x\)
  8. (8) \(\frac{d}{d x}(\cot x)=-\operatorname{cosec}^{2} x\)
  9. (9) \(\frac{d}{d x}(\sec x)=\sec x \tan x\)
  10. (10) \(\frac{d}{d x}(\operatorname{cosec} x)=-\operatorname{cosec} x \cot x\)

Results:

  1. 1) If u and v are functions of x, \(\frac{d}{d x}(u \pm v)=\frac{d u}{d x} \pm \frac{d v}{d x}\)
  2. 2) \(\frac{d}{d x}(k u)=k \frac{d u}{d x}\), where k is a constant.
  3. 3) \(\frac{d}{d x}(\text { any constant })=0\)
  4. 4) Product Rule of Differentiation
    If u & v are two functions of x,
    \(\frac{d}{d x}(u v)=u \frac{d v}{d x}+v \frac{d u}{d x}\)
  5. 5) \(\frac{d}{d x}(u v w)=u v \frac{d w}{d x}+v w \frac{d u}{d x}+w u \frac{d v}{d x}\), where u, v and w are function of x.
  6. 6) Quotient Rule of Differentiation
    If u & v are functions of x,
    \(\frac{d}{d x}\left(\frac{u}{v}\right)=\frac{v \frac{d u}{d x}-u \frac{d v}{d x}}{v^{2}}\)

WORKED EXAMPLES

PART - A

1. Find \(\frac{d y}{d x}\) if

(i) \(y=\frac{1}{x^{2}}\)
(ii) \(y=x^{3}+2\)
(iii) \(y=\frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}\)
(iv) \(y=\sqrt{x}\)
(v) \(y=\frac{1}{\sin x}\)
(vi) \(y=9 \sqrt{x}+x^{2}\)

Solution:

(i) \(y=\frac{1}{x^{2}}=x^{-2}\)
\(\therefore \frac{d y}{d x}=\frac{d}{d x}\left(x^{-2}\right)=-2 x^{-3}=-\frac{2}{x^{3}}\)

(ii) \(y=x^{3}+2\)
\(\therefore \frac{d y}{d x}=\frac{d}{d x}\left(x^{3}+2\right)=\frac{d}{d x}\left(x^{3}\right)+\frac{d}{d x}(2)=3 x^{2}+0=3 x^{2}\)

(iii) \(y=\frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}=\frac{1}{x^{1 / 2}}=x^{-1 / 2}\)
\(\therefore \frac{d y}{d x}=\frac{d}{d x}\left(x^{-1 / 2}\right)=-\frac{1}{2} x^{-\frac{1}{2}-1}=-\frac{1}{2} x^{-3 / 2}=-\frac{1}{2 x^{3 / 2}}\)

(iv) \(y=\sqrt{x}=x^{1 / 2}\)
\(\frac{d y}{d x}=\frac{d}{d x}\left(x^{1 / 2}\right)=\frac{1}{2} x^{\frac{1}{2}-1}=\frac{1}{2} x^{-1 / 2}=\frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{1}{x^{1 / 2}}=\frac{1}{2 \sqrt{x}}\)

(v) \(y=\frac{1}{\sin x}=\operatorname{cosec} x\)
\(\therefore \frac{d y}{d x}=\frac{d}{d x}(\operatorname{cosec} x)=-\operatorname{cosec} x \cot x\)

(vi) \(y=9 \sqrt{x}+x^{2}\)
\(\therefore \frac{d y}{d x}=\frac{d}{d x}\left(9 \sqrt{x}+x^{2}\right)=9 \frac{d}{d x}(\sqrt{x})+\frac{d}{d x}\left(x^{2}\right)\)
\( =9 \cdot \frac{1}{2 \sqrt{x}}+2 x \)
\( =\frac{9}{2 \sqrt{x}}+2 x \)

PART -B

1. Find \(\frac{d y}{d x}\) if \(y=\frac{3}{x^{2}}+\frac{2}{x}+\frac{1}{4}\).

Solution:

\(y =\frac{3}{x^{2}}+\frac{2}{x}+\frac{1}{4}=3 x^{-2}+2 x^{-1}+\frac{1}{4}\)
\(\frac{d y}{d x} =\frac{d}{d x}\left(3 x^{-2}+2 x^{-1}+\frac{1}{4}\right)=3(-2) x^{-3}+2(-1) x^{-2}+0\)
\(=\frac{-6}{x^{3}}-\frac{2}{x^{2}}\)

2. Find \(\frac{d y}{d x}\) if \(y=e^{x} \sin x\).

Solution:

\(y =e^{x} \sin x\)
\(\frac{d y}{d x} =\frac{d}{d x}\left(e^{x} \sin x\right)=" u \frac{d v}{d x}+v \frac{d u}{d x} "\)
\( =e^{x} \frac{d}{d x}(\sin x)+\sin x \frac{d}{d x}\left(e^{x}\right) \)
\( =e^{x} \cos x+\sin x \cdot e^{x} \)

3. Find \(\frac{d y}{d x}\) if \(y=x^{2} e^{x} \sin x\).

Solution:

\(y =x^{2} e^{x} \sin x\)
\(\frac{d y}{d x} =\frac{d}{d x}\left(x^{2} e^{x} \sin x\right)=" u v \frac{d w}{d x}+v w \frac{d u}{d x}+w u \frac{d v}{d x} "\)
\( =x^{2} e^{x} \frac{d}{d x}(\sin x)+e^{x} \sin x \frac{d}{d x}\left(x^{2}\right)+\sin x \cdot x^{2} \frac{d}{d x}\left(e^{x}\right) \)
\( =x^{2} e^{x} \cos x+e^{x} \sin x \cdot 2 x+\sin x \cdot x^{2} e^{x} \)

4. Find \(\frac{d y}{d x}\) if \(y=\frac{\cos x}{\sqrt{x}}\).

Solution:

\(y =\frac{\cos x}{\sqrt{x}}\)
\(\frac{d y}{d x} =\frac{v \frac{d u}{d x}-u \frac{d v}{d x}}{v^{2}}\)
\(=\frac{\sqrt{x} \frac{d}{d x}(\cos x)-\cos x \frac{d}{d x}(\sqrt{x})}{(\sqrt{x})^{2}}\)
\(=\frac{\sqrt{x}(-\sin x)-\cos x \cdot \frac{1}{2 \sqrt{x}}}{x}\)

PART - C

1. Find \(\frac{d y}{d x}\) if \(y=\left(x^{2}+3\right) \cos x \log x\).

Solution:

\(y =\left(x^{2}+3\right) \cos x \log x\)
\(\frac{d y}{d x} =\frac{d}{d x}\left[\left(x^{2}+3\right) \cos x \log x\right]=" u v \frac{d w}{d x}+v w \frac{d u}{d x}+w u \frac{d v}{d x} "\)
\(=\left(x^{2}+3\right) \cos x \frac{d}{d x}(\log x)+\cos x \log x \frac{d}{d x}\left(x^{2}+3\right)+\log x\left(x^{2}+3\right) \frac{d}{d x}(\cos x)\)
\(=\left(x^{2}+3\right) \cos x \cdot \frac{1}{x}+\cos x \log x \cdot 2 x+\log x \cdot\left(x^{2}+3\right)(-\sin x)\)
\(=\frac{\left(x^{2}+3\right) \cos x}{x}+2 x \cos x \log x-\left(x^{2}+3\right) \sin x \log x\)

2. If \(y=\frac{x^{3} \tan x}{e^{x}+1}\), find \(\frac{d y}{d x}\).

Solution:

\(y =\frac{x^{3} \tan x}{e^{x}+1}\)
\(\frac{d y}{d x} =\frac{v \frac{d u}{d x}-u \frac{d v}{d x}}{v^{2}}\)
\(=\frac{\left(e^{x}+1\right) \frac{d}{d x}\left(x^{3} \tan x\right)-\left(x^{3} \tan x\right) \frac{d}{d x}\left(e^{x}+1\right)}{\left(e^{x}+1\right)^{2}}\)
\(=\frac{\left(e^{x}+1\right)\left[x^{3} \frac{d}{d x}(\tan x)+\tan x \frac{d}{d x}\left(x^{3}\right)\right]-x^{3} \tan x \cdot e^{x}}{\left(e^{x}+1\right)^{2}}\)
\(=\frac{\left(e^{x}+1\right)\left[x^{3} \sec ^{2} x+\tan x \cdot 3 x^{2}\right]-x^{3} \tan x \cdot e^{x}}{\left(e^{x}+1\right)^{2}}\)

3. Find \(\frac{d y}{d x}\) if \(y=\frac{1+x+x^{2}}{1-x+x^{2}}\).

Solution:

\(y =\frac{1+x+x^{2}}{1-x+x^{2}}\)
\(\frac{d y}{d x} =\frac{v \frac{d u}{d x}-u \frac{d v}{d x}}{v^{2}}\)
\(=\frac{\left(1-x+x^{2}\right) \frac{d}{d x}\left(1+x+x^{2}\right)-\left(1+x+x^{2}\right) \frac{d}{d x}\left(1-x+x^{2}\right)}{\left(1-x+x^{2}\right)^{2}}\)
\(=\frac{\left(1-x+x^{2}\right)(1+2 x)-\left(1+x+x^{2}\right)(-1+2 x)}{\left(1-x+x^{2}\right)^{2}}\)
\(=\frac{(1+x+2x^2-2x^3) - (-1+x+x^2+2x^3)}{(1-x+x^2)^2}\)
\(=\frac{(1+x+2x^2-2x^3) + (1-x-x^2-2x^3)}{(1-x+x^2)^2}\)
\(=\frac{2+x^2-4x^3}{\left(1-x+x^{2}\right)^{2}}\)
