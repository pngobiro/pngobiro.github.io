Workshop FULL Notes

MATERIALS, PROCESSES AND WORKSHOP TECHNOLOGY.

  • TOPIC 1: OCCUPATIONAL SAFETY

    • Safety regulations, causes of accidents, electrical safety, fire safety.

  • TOPIC 2: MATERIALS AND PROCESSES.

    • Metals, non-metals, alloys, extraction processes, properties, finishes.

  • TOPIC 3: METAL SHOP TOOLS AND MEASUREMENT.

    • Measurement terms, marking out techniques, hand tools (vices, files, saws, hammers, chisels, snips, taps, dies).

  • TOPIC 4: JOINING OF METALS

    • Mechanical joining (temporary, screws, bolts, nuts).

  • TOPIC 5: WORKSHOP MACHINES AND APPLICATIONS.

    • Drilling machine, hand drill, power drill, center lathe, shaping machine, grinding machine.
