üí° Definition

Ethics refers to the principles of rights and wrong that can be used by individuals acting as free moral agents to make choices to guide their behavior. Information systems raise new ethical questions for both individuals and society because they create opportunities for intense social change and threaten existing distribution of power, money, rights and obligations.
