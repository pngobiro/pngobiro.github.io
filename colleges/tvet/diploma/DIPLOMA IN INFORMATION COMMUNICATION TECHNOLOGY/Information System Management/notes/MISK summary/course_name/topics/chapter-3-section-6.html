Executive Information Systems in Detail

🎯 Role of EIS

  • 📊 Provides easy to use desktop analytical tools and online data displays
  • 💻 Provides managers with user-friendly software tools for analytical purposes
  • 🔄 Integrates data from different systems for comprehensive analysis
  • 🌐 Provides external data like stock market news, industry trends, and competitor information

📋 Characteristics of EIS

  • ⚡ Immediate and easy access to key success factor information
  • 🎨 User-seductive interface with color graphics and video
  • 🔍 Drill-down capabilities for detailed data analysis
  • 🔄 Access to various internal and external databases
  • 📊 Provides current status and data projections
  • ⚙️ Easy customization for different users

🔧 Elements of Successful EIS

  • 📄 Standard Reports with easy navigation
  • 🔍 Drill-down capabilities
  • ⚡ Quick issue management
  • 📊 Exceptional reporting capabilities
  • 📰 Current and focused electronic news delivery
  • 📧 Executive mail with minimal keyboard interaction
  • ⏰ Time management tools
  • 💾 Access to corporate and public databases

⚖️ Benefits and Problems

✅

Benefits

  • ⏱️ Saves staff and executive time
  • 🔍 Improves understanding of company and environment
  • 🔄 Eliminates communication bottlenecks
  • 📊 Provides summarized and custom-tailored data
  • 📈 Ensures clearer and up-to-date information
⚠️

Problems

  • 🎯 Executive success/failure has major organizational impact
  • 👥 Manager discomfort with executive access to operational data
  • 🔒 Risk of data being withheld from databases
