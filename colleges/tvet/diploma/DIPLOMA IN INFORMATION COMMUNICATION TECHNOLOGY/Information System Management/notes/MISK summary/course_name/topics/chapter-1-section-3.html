Core Concepts

The processing of data into information and communicating the resulting information to the user are the very essence of an MIS. Data is the term for collections of facts and figures: hours worked, invoice values, part numbers, usage rates, items received, etc. These basic facts are stored, analyzed, compared, calculated and generally worked on to produce messages in the form required by the user i.e. the manager, which is then termed information.
