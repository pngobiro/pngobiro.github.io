Answer

Disclaimer: Answers are AI-generated and may contain errors. Verify independently.

(e) Sort by 'Time needed' (Col B). Data > Subtotal. 'At each change in': Time needed, 'Use function': Sum, 'Add subtotal to': Total price. OK. Copy Sheet1 to Sheet2. Rename Sheet1 to `budget`, Sheet2 to `chart`.

(f) Go to `chart` sheet. Remove subtotals (Data > Subtotal > Remove All). Select data for chart (likely the subtotal rows generated in step e.i, or recreate summary). Insert > Chart > Bar > Clustered Bar. Add Title: "budget totals". Add Legend > Bottom.

(g) Save. Prepare `budget` and `chart` sheets for printing.
