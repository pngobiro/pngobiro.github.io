Answer

Disclaimer: Answers are AI-generated and may contain errors. Verify independently.

(a) Open Excel. Enter title in A1 (merged A1:D1). Enter headers A2:D2. Enter data A3:C11. Save as `Budget.xlsx`.

(b) Total Cost (D3): `=B3*C3`. Drag down to D11. Total (D13): `=SUM(D3:D11)`. Enter label in A13.

(c) Right-click Sheet1 tab > Move or Copy > Create a copy > OK. Rename Sheet1 to `AFybudget`. Rename Sheet1 (2) to `copy`.

(d) In `copy` sheet, find an empty cell (e.g., F3). Formula: `=COUNTIF(D3:D11,">750")`.

(e) In `copy` sheet, select Items (A3:A11) and Total Cost (D3:D11). Insert > Charts > Pie > Pie. Add Title. Add Data Labels (e.g., Category Name, Value/Percentage). Position chart.

(f) Save. Go to `AFybudget` sheet. Formulas > Show Formulas. Prepare `AFybudget` (formulas) and `copy` (chart) sheets for printing.
