Answer

Disclaimer: Answers are AI-generated and may contain errors. Verify independently.

(a) Open Excel. Enter data A1:C10 (adjusting for merged cells in header). Save as `CHcases.xlsx`. Select A3:E3. Format Cells > Alignment > Orientation: 45 degrees.

(b)

  1. Total Cases (D4): `=SUM(B4:C4)`. Drag down to D10.
  2. Weekly Total (B12): `=SUM(B4:B10)`. Weekly Total (C12): `=SUM(C4:C10)`. Weekly Total (D12): `=SUM(D4:D10)`.
  3. Percent (E4): `=D4/$D$12`. Drag down to E10. Format E4:E10 as Percentage, 2 decimal places.

(c)

  1. Over 200 Morning (B13): `=COUNTIF(B4:B10,">200")`.
  2. Total >500 Cases (D13): `=SUMIF(D4:D10,">500")`.

(d) Select Days (A4:A10), Morning Cases (B4:B10), Afternoon Cases (C4:C10). Insert > Chart > Bar > Clustered Bar. Move Chart > New Sheet. Name: `Chart Sheet`. Add Title: "Reported Cases".

(e) Save. Prepare Sheet1 and Chart Sheet for printing.

(a) Open a presentation program and create the slides as they appear in figure 3 using appropriate slide layouts. Save the presentation as Hypotest in the KNECEXAM folder. (15.5 marks)

(Note: Content for slides 1-3 is derived from the image in Figure 3)

  1. Slide 1: Title Slide - HYPOTHESIS TESTING, Subtitle: BY: PROFESSOR JK
  2. Slide 2: Title and Content - Introduction, Bullet points: The Null Hypothesis, Type I and Type II Error, Using Statistics..., The Logic...
  3. Slide 3: Title and Content - Research question:, Bullet points for Non-directional, Example, Hypothesis:, Bullet points for Statement, Directionality, Example.

(b) Insert the following as footer to all the slides:

  1. the text "Hypothesis Testing" to the left,
  2. the current Date to the right.

(c) Apply the following to all slides:

  1. transition effect of your choice;
  2. transition speed: slow;
  3. Slide design of your choice.

(d) Save the changes to print out later hypotest as handout with three slides per page. (1 mark)

Answer

Disclaimer: Answers are AI-generated and may contain errors. Verify independently.

(a) Open PowerPoint. Create slides 1-3 based on Figure 3 content. Use Title Slide and Title & Content layouts. Save as `Hypotest.pptx`.

(b) Insert > Header & Footer. Check 'Date and time' (select Update automatically for current date). Check 'Footer', type "Hypothesis Testing". Apply to All. *(Note: Aligning left/right might require editing the slide master)*.
