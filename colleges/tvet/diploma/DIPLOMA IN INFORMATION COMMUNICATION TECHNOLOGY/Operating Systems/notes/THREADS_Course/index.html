Operating Systems: Threads and Process Management

Duration: 4 hours Level: Intermediate

Course Overview

This course covers essential concepts in operating systems, focusing on threads, process synchronization, and CPU scheduling. Students will learn about thread management, synchronization mechanisms, and scheduling algorithms.
