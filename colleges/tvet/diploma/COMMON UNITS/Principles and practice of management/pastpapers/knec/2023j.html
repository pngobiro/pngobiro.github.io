Question 1 (20 marks)

(a) Describe five indicators of effective management in an organization. (10 marks)

(b) Outline five benefits of applying the Human Relations Theory in an organization. (10 marks)

Answer

(a) Five indicators of effective management in an organization:
  1. Achievement of organizational goals
    • Meeting or exceeding set targets
    • Timely completion of projects
    • High quality outputs
  2. Employee satisfaction and motivation
    • Low staff turnover rates
    • High employee engagement
    • Positive workplace culture
  3. Resource optimization
    • Efficient use of available resources
    • Cost-effective operations
    • Minimal waste
  4. Customer satisfaction
    • Positive customer feedback
    • Repeat business
    • Growing customer base
  5. Growth and innovation
    • Continuous improvement
    • Adaptation to market changes
    • Development of new products/services
(b) Five benefits of applying Human Relations Theory in an organization:
  1. Enhanced employee motivation
    • Recognition of social needs
    • Focus on intrinsic rewards
    • Greater job satisfaction
  2. Improved communication
    • Better vertical and horizontal communication
    • Open feedback channels
    • Reduced misunderstandings
  3. Higher productivity
    • Motivated workforce
    • Better teamwork
    • Increased employee engagement
  4. Better workplace relationships
    • Stronger team bonds
    • Improved collaboration
    • Positive work environment
  5. Lower employee turnover
    • Higher job satisfaction
    • Greater organizational commitment
    • Reduced recruitment costs

    • Sets performance benchmarks
    • Measures progress
  5. Enhances organizational identity

    • Communicates organizational culture
