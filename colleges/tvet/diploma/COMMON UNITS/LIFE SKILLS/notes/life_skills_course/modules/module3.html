Skills for Positive Relationships

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Explain the various skills under this category.
  • Managing cooperation, conflict, and persuasion.

Skills under this category include:

  1. Negotiation and conflict management skills
  2. Assertiveness skills
  3. Empathy
  4. Cooperation and team work
  5. Advocacy skills- that is skill of influence and persuasion (interpersonal relations)

📝 Summary

  • Skills for positive relationships involve negotiation, conflict management, assertiveness, empathy, and cooperation.
  • Effective advocacy skills enhance interpersonal relations.
