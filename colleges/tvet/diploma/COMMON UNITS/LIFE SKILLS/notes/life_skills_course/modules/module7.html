Effective Communication

Effective communication entails sending accurate information and receiving feedback that the message has been received without distortion. Acquisition of this skill enables people to overcome their inhibitions, communicate effectively and maintain healthy social relationships.

  1. a) Communication process Communication begins with a message that is developed at the source. The source channels the message to the receiver. The receiver then sends feedback to the sender. Communication process has the following components:
