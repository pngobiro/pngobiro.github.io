Life Skills Course

Duration: Approximately 20 hours Level: Beginner/Intermediate

Course Overview

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to essential life skills, covering a wide range of topics from self-awareness and stress management to communication, conflict resolution, and decision-making. It is designed to equip learners with the abilities needed to navigate the challenges of everyday life effectively.
