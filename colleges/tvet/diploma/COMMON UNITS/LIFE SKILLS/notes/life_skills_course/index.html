Life Skills Course

Duration: Approximately 20 hours Level: Beginner/Intermediate

Course Overview

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to essential life skills, covering a wide range of topics from self-awareness and stress management to communication, conflict resolution, and decision-making. It is designed to equip learners with the abilities needed to navigate the challenges of everyday life effectively.

🎯 Course Objectives

  • Develop self-awareness and understand personal strengths and weaknesses.
  • Learn effective stress management techniques.
  • Improve communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Master conflict resolution and negotiation strategies.
  • Enhance critical and creative thinking abilities.
  • Understand the importance of time management and goal setting.
  • Gain knowledge about child labor, human rights, and gender education.
