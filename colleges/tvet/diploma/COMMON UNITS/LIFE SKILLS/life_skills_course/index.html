Life Skills KNEC Diploma Notes

Duration: Estimated 10-12 hours Level: Diploma

Course Overview

This course provides comprehensive notes on Life Skills, tailored for the KNEC Diploma syllabus. It covers a wide range of topics, from self-awareness and goal setting to conflict resolution and understanding human rights.

🎯 Course Objectives

  • Develop an understanding of essential life skills concepts.
  • Learn practical strategies for self-improvement and interpersonal relationships.
  • Gain knowledge of key areas such as stress management, emotional intelligence, and time management.
  • Understand human rights and responsible citizenship.
