Business Communication

Estimated Duration: 15 hours Level: Beginner/Intermediate

Course Overview

This course is designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and attitude to communicate effectively in an organization.

Course Modules

  1. Module 1: Introduction to Business Communication

    Covers the overview, characteristics, elements, process of communication, and organizational communication.

    4 hours

  2. Module 2: Barriers and Oral/Nonverbal Communication

    Focuses on communication barriers, oral communication (including interviews and meetings), and nonverbal communication.

    4 hours

  3. Module 3: Listening and Written Communication

    Covers active listening and written business communication, including memos, letters, and other forms.

    4 hours

  4. Module 4: Reports and Visual Communication

    Explains different types of reports and visual communication techniques, including presentations.

    3 hours

Prerequisites

  • Basic reading and writing skills in English.
