Entrepreneurship KNEC Diploma Notes

Duration: Estimated 20 hours Level: Diploma

Course Overview

This Entrepreneurship KNEC Diploma Notes course provides a comprehensive introduction to entrepreneurship, covering essential concepts, theories, and practical skills needed to start and manage a small business effectively. The course is divided into modules that systematically build your understanding of entrepreneurship, from its fundamental principles to advanced management techniques and emerging trends.

🎯 Course Objectives

  • Understand the fundamentals of entrepreneurship and its role in national development.
  • Explore the evolution of entrepreneurship globally and in Kenya.
  • Identify and analyze the characteristics and roles of an entrepreneur.
  • Foster creativity and innovation in a business context.
  • Comprehend entrepreneurial culture and its development.
  • Generate and evaluate viable business opportunities.
  • Grasp the motivational factors driving entrepreneurs.
  • Develop key entrepreneurial competencies for effective business management.
  • Learn the procedures and factors involved in starting a small business.
  • Master essential business enterprise management functions.
  • Understand financial management principles and practices.
  • Apply marketing strategies for business success.
  • Recognize and implement enterprise social responsibilities and ethical practices.
  • Utilize Information Communication Technology (ICT) in business operations.
  • Analyze and adapt to emerging issues and trends in entrepreneurship.
