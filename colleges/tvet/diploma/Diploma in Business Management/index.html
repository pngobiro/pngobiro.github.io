Office Administration and Management

Duration: 2 Terms Level: Diploma

Term 1

  1. Introduction to Office Administration and Management
  2. Organizational Structures and Departments
  3. Office Organization
  4. The Office
  5. The Office Personnel
  6. Office Furniture and Stationery

Term 2

  1. Handling Office Correspondence
  2. Filing and Storage of Records
  3. Reprography
  4. Organization and Methods

Course Objectives

  • Understand fundamental concepts of office administration and management
  • Master organizational structures and departmental functions
  • Learn effective office organization and personnel management
  • Develop skills in handling office correspondence and records
  • Understand modern office systems and methods

Prerequisites

  • Basic understanding of business operations
  • Good communication skills
