The Office

Definition and Purpose

An office may be defined as any place set aside for work of a clerical nature. It serves as an administrative center of an organization comprising departments responsible for their respective functions.

Functions of an Office

Basic Functions

  • Receiving information
  • Recording information
  • Preparing and arranging information
  • Giving information

Administrative Functions

  • Form design and control
  • Selection and purchase of office appliances
  • Personnel functions
  • Safeguarding assets
  • Planning schemes and policies
  • Maintenance of records
  • Controlling office costs
  • Development of office systems and procedures
  • Public relations

Importance of the Office

  • Offers valuable service to all other departments
  • Acts as an information and memory center
  • Serves as an intermediary
  • Functions as a coordinator
  • Acts as an administrative nerve center
  • Serves as a control center

Office Environment

Essential Environmental Factors

  • Lighting and ventilation
  • Temperature control
  • Noise management
  • Safety measures
  • Sanitation and cleanliness

Noise Control Measures

Internal Noise Sources

  • Machine movements
  • Movement and conversation of staff
  • Creaking doors
  • Calling bells and telephones
  • Furniture movement

Solutions for Internal Noise

  • Use of carpets or rubber mats
  • Felt pads under noisy equipment
  • Replace bells with buzzers
  • Sound-proof telephone booths
  • Staff guidelines for maintaining quietness
  • Proper door fittings

Office Safety and Security

Safety Measures

  • Provision of safe working space
  • Safeguarding hazardous equipment
  • Provision of safety clothing
  • Maintaining orderliness
  • First aid facilities
  • Fire fighting equipment
  • Safety training

Security Measures

  • Access control for visitors
  • Secure document handling
  • Staff identification systems
  • Regular security checks
  • Protection of confidential information

Key Points Summary

  • The office serves as a central hub for organizational operations
  • Both basic and administrative functions are crucial for success
  • Environmental factors significantly impact work efficiency
  • Safety and security measures protect both people and assets
  • Regular maintenance of office environment ensures productivity
