The Office Personnel

The Office Manager

The office manager is the person in charge with responsibility to run the office efficiently.

Duties of an Office Manager

  • Ensures smooth flow of work in the office
  • Chooses or advises on selecting machines and other assets
  • Trains the office staff
  • Advises top management on office-related matters
  • Advocates for staff welfare, promotions, etc.
  • Develops office plans and policies
  • Motivates staff towards achieving company objectives

Characteristics of a Good Office Manager

  • Strong leadership skills
  • Sound judgment
  • Methodical approach
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Strong character
  • Disciplined
  • Politeness
  • Forward-looking mindset

Qualities of Office Personnel

Personal Qualities

  • Good personal hygiene
  • Attractive personality
  • Personal interests aligned with role
  • Appropriate qualifications and training

Office Etiquette

Office etiquette refers to the code of professional conduct or social behavior.

  • Honesty and loyalty to the organization
  • Well-disciplined behavior
  • Polite and respectful interaction with colleagues and customers
  • Respect for seniors
  • Punctuality and regularity

Clerical Activities

These activities include keeping records and preparing accounts. Common classifications include:

  • Communication
  • Calculations
  • Records management
  • Report preparation
  • Routine procedures

Human Relations in the Office

Ways to Build Good Relations

  • Complete work on schedule
  • Follow instructions carefully
  • Report work-related difficulties promptly
  • Request assistance when needed
  • Offer help where appropriate
  • Maintain courtesy in all interactions
  • Provide feedback to confirm understanding
  • Adhere to policies and regulations

Showing Consideration for Others

  • Share resources and information fairly
  • Minimize unnecessary noise
  • Support colleagues during absences
  • Show empathy and understanding
  • Respect others' opinions
  • Maintain professional boundaries

Key Points Summary

  • Office managers play a crucial role in organizational success
  • Personal qualities and professional etiquette are essential
  • Effective human relations contribute to workplace harmony
  • Clear understanding of roles and responsibilities is important
  • Continuous professional development enhances performance
