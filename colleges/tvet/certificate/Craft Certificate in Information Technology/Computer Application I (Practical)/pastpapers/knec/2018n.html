TASK 1 (Word Processing) (15 marks)

Maji Mazuri Technical has invited a motivational speaker to give a talk on life skills. You have been asked to prepare the notes.

(a) Open a word processing program and key in the following information as it appears. Save it as lifeskills in the KNECEXAM folder.

This refers to the skills needed to make the most out of life. Life skills are usually associated with managing and living a better quality life. They help us to accomplish our full potential.

Any skill that is useful in your life can be considered a life skill. By learning new skills we increase our understanding of the world around us and equip ourselves with the relevant tools to cope with the challenges that life throws at us.

Essential Life Skills

Certain skills may be more or less relevant to you depending on life circumstances, your culture, beliefs, age, geographic location, etc. Different life skills will be more or less relevant at different times our lives, for example;

  1. Study skills: needed when at school or college.
  2. Negotiation skills: needed when buying something like cloths and other basic necessities.
  3. Employability skills: needed when searching for a job.
  4. Time management and organizing skills: are important in everyday life of an individual.

(b) Apply the following formats in the document created in (a):

  1. Sub title "essential life skills": Verdana and size 18.
  2. Line spacing 1.5.

(c) Insert an in margin drop cap to the first letter of the first paragraph.

(d) Insert a picture of your choice in the third paragraph.

  1. Apply the text wrapping style of tight to the picture.
  2. Resize the picture to only stretch within the paragraph.

(e) Save changes to print out later.

Answer

(Practical Task: Word Processing)

Document Creation & Formatting:

  1. Create and save document as 'lifeskills'
  2. Type provided content with correct structure
  3. Format "essential life skills" (Verdana, size 18)
  4. Set 1.5 line spacing
  5. Add drop cap to first paragraph
  6. Insert and format picture in third paragraph

Printout Required: lifeskills document
