Quiz: Topic 6 - Financial Management in Learning Institutions

Test your knowledge on the concepts from Topic 6.

1. Which of the following are the four stages of the financial management cycle as described in the text?

  • A) Hiring, Training, Promoting, and Retiring
  • B) Planning and budgeting, Resource allocation, Operations and monitoring, and Evaluation and reporting
  • C) Marketing, Sales, Delivery, and Support
  • D) Spending, Saving, Investing, and Auditing

Answer: B) Planning and budgeting, Resource allocation, Operations and monitoring, and Evaluation and reporting.

Explanation: The text explicitly lists these four components as the critical parts of the financial management cycle.

2. Which book of accounts is referred to as the "final entry book" where summarized journal entries are tracked to get reconciled balances?

  • A) General Journal
  • B) Cash Receipt Journal
  • C) General Ledger
  • D) Sales Journal

Answer: C) General Ledger.

Explanation: The text defines the General Ledger as the "final entry book" where you "see the summarized journal entries of an account to get the reconciled balances."

3. True or False: A supplementary budget is created when a school has excess funds and needs to spend them quickly.

Answer: False.

Explanation: A supplementary budget is a request for additional funding for activities or expenditures that were "not adequately covered or anticipated in the original budget."

4. What is a crucial first step in the "Taking Over and Handing Over" process?

  • A) Immediately changing all the passwords.
  • B) Deleting all files from the previous occupant.
  • C) Discussing the handover with a manager and preparing a formal handover document.
  • D) Taking a long vacation to prepare for the new role.

Answer: C) Discussing the handover with a manager and preparing a formal handover document.

Explanation: The text emphasizes the importance of starting with a detailed discussion with your manager and drawing up a handover document to ensure a smooth transition.

5. Fill in the blank: The goal of Change Management is to transition smoothly and __________, helping employees understand and adapt to new ways of working.

Answer: efficiently.

Explanation: The text states, "The goal is to transition smoothly and efficiently. It's best if changes can be planned to avoid disruptions and employee resistance."
