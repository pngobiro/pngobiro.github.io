Quiz: Topic 3 - Basic Education in Kenya

Test your knowledge on the concepts from Topic 3.

1. What is one of the primary functions of the Ministry of Education in Kenya regarding the school curriculum?

  • A) Designing the curriculum from scratch.
  • B) Printing and selling all textbooks.
  • C) Revising and approving the curriculum designed by KICD.
  • D) Directly training all teachers on the new curriculum.

Answer: C) Revising and approving the curriculum designed by KICD.

Explanation: The text states that the "Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development (KICD) designs the curriculum and forwards it to the education ministry for approval."

2. According to the text, which of the following is considered a 'Passive' agency of education?

  • A) The family
  • B) The school
  • C) The peer group
  • D) The library

Answer: D) The library.

Explanation: Passive agencies are those that affect the individual but are not affected by them. The text lists the library, radio, and television as examples of passive agencies.

3. What is a key function of the County Education Board in Kenya?

  • A) Appointing the Cabinet Secretary of Education.
  • B) Directly managing the finances of all national universities.
  • C) Overseeing the operation of youth polytechnics and pre-primary education in the county.
  • D) Setting the questions for the national examinations.

Answer: C) Overseeing the operation of youth polytechnics and pre-primary education in the county.

Explanation: The text lists one of the primary functions of the County Education Board as overseeing "the operation and management of youth polytechnics, pre-primary education including early childhood care and education programmes in the county".

4. True or False: The family is classified as a formal and passive agency of education.

Answer: False.

Explanation: The text classifies the family as an informal and active agency of education, being the "primary agent of education" where there is direct personal interaction.

5. Fill in the blank: A group of individuals of a similar age and status that plays a significant role in socialization and cultural development is known as a _________.

Answer: peer group.

Explanation: The text defines a peer group as "a group of individuals having more or less same age and status" and lists socialization as one of its key educational roles.
