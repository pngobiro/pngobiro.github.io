Quiz: Topic 2 - Legal Provisions in Basic Education

Test your knowledge on the concepts from Topic 2.

1. Which Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) is specifically focused on ensuring "inclusive and equitable quality education"?

  • A) SDG 1: No Poverty
  • B) SDG 5: Gender Equality
  • C) SDG 4: Quality Education
  • D) SDG 16: Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions

Answer: C) SDG 4: Quality Education.

Explanation: The text explicitly identifies SDG 4 as the goal to "Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all".

2. According to the Kenyan Constitution, which article states that "every child has a right to free and compulsory basic education"?

  • A) Article 24
  • B) Article 53 (1) (b)
  • C) Article 55 (a)
  • D) Article 6

Answer: B) Article 53 (1) (b).

Explanation: The text states, "The Constitution of Kenya, in Article 53 (1) (b) state that every child has a right to free and compulsory basic education...".

3. True or False: The East African Community (EAC) Harmonization Framework is designed to limit the movement of learners and workers between partner states.

Answer: False.

Explanation: The framework's objectives include expanding "opportunities for mobility between countries, institutions and education levels" and promoting "workers and learners mobility".

4. What is a primary purpose of the Basic Education Act of 2013?

  • A) To regulate university tuition fees.
  • B) To promote and regulate free and compulsory basic education.
  • C) To outline the rules for private sector businesses only.
  • D) To manage national sports teams.

Answer: B) To promote and regulate free and compulsory basic education.

Explanation: The text states that the Basic Education Act no 14 of 2013 is an act of parliament that seeks to "Promote and regulate free and compulsory basic education."

5. Fill in the blank: One of the key benefits of having well-developed legal policies in a school is to provide a form of ________ control to limit behavior and guide decision-making.

Answer: internal.

Explanation: The section on the importance of legal documents states, "Organizational policies should serve as an important form of internal control. Policy form as a control mechanism that will effectively limit the behaviour of teaching and non-teaching staff."
