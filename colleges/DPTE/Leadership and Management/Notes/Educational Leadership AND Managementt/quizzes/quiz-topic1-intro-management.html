Quiz: Topic 1 - Educational Leadership and Management

Test your knowledge on the concepts from Topic 1.

1. What is the primary motivation for instructional leadership according to the text?

  • A) Managing school budgets effectively.
  • B) The improvement of instruction.
  • C) Distributing leadership roles to all teachers.
  • D) Establishing a strict and orderly school environment.

Answer: B) The improvement of instruction.

Explanation: The text explicitly states, "The motivation for instructional leadership is the improvement of instruction".

2. True or False: Instructional leadership is solely the responsibility of the school principal.

Answer: False.

Explanation: The text explains that instructional leadership is "distributed through networks of influence" and that "middle leaders and other teachers who have responsibility for teaching and learning" also play a key role.

3. Which leadership style is described as the inverse of democratic leadership, where the leader makes decisions without input from their reports?

  • A) Laissez-Faire Leadership
  • B) Transformational Leadership
  • C) Autocratic Leadership
  • D) Strategic Leadership

Answer: C) Autocratic Leadership.

Explanation: The text defines Autocratic leadership as "the inverse of democratic leadership. In this leadership style, the leader makes decisions without taking input from anyone who reports to them."

4. Fill in the blank: The French term "laissez-faire" literally translates to "______".

Answer: let them do.

Explanation: The section on Laissez-Faire Leadership states, "The French term 'laissez-faire' literally translates to 'let them do.'"

5. According to the text, what is one of the key purposes of educational planning?

  • A) To only respond to parental complaints.
  • B) To ensure global competitiveness and actualize government political philosophy.
  • C) To reduce the number of teachers in a school.
  • D) To focus solely on extracurricular activities.

Answer: B) To ensure global competitiveness and actualize government political philosophy.

Explanation: The section on planning lists several purposes, including "To ensure global competitiveness and more importantly, To actualize government political philosophy."
