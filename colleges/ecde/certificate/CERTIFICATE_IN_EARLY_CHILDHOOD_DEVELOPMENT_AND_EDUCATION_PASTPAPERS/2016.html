2016

CERTIFICATE IN EARLY CHILDHOOD DEVELOPMENT AND EDUCATION - 2016

Paper: 7012 Exam Body: THE KENYA NATIONAL EXAMINATIONS COUNCIL

Instructions

Write your name and index number in the spaces provided above. Sign and write the date of the examination in the spaces provided above. This question paper consists of TWO sections; A and B. Answer ALL the questions in section A and any FOUR questions from section B. Answers to both section A and B must be written in the spaces provided in this booklet. Do NOT remove any pages from this booklet. Candidates should answer the questions in English.

Questions

Question 1

   a) Define the term data as used in Research. (2 marks)

   b) Give four advantages of using frequency tables for data presentation in Research. (4 marks)

   c) Outline four benefits to a trainee who uses observation method when collecting data in an Early Childhood Development and Education (ECDE) centre. (4 marks)

Question 2

   a) Distinguish between capacity building and community empowerment. (4 marks)

   b) State four elements of a communication process between an ECDE teacher and the community. (4 marks)

   c) List two characteristics of Laissez-Faire leadership style. (2 marks)

Question 3

   a) Explain the term General Knowledge. (2 marks)

   b) Give five reasons that make integrity an important value for a pre-school teacher in the society. (5 marks)

   c) State three characteristics of a socialism type of Government. (3 marks)

Question 4

   a) Give four reasons for maintaining permanent store records in an ECDE centre. (4 marks)

   b) Identity the type of information that a Sub-County Early Childhood Coordinator would find in each of the following records kept in an ECDE centre: (i) log book; (3 marks) (ii) visitors book. (3 marks)

Question 5

   a) Discuss four roles of a pre-school teacher in ensuring that children's right to medical care is attained through immunization in Kenya. (8 marks)

   b) Outline eight qualities of pre-school teaching as a profession. (7 marks)

Question 6

   a) Identify seven ways in which the County Governments would effectively participate in community capacity building and mobilization of ECDE programmes in Kenya. (7 marks)

   b) Examine four disadvantages of using indirect mode of communication when mobilizing the community to support an ECDE project. (8 marks)

Question 7

   a) Highlight seven characteristics of a Democratic leader. (7 marks)

   b) Outline eight factors that hinder the achievement of Education for All (EFA) in ECDE in Kenya. (8 marks)

Question 8

   a) Identify seven services provided by the Higher Education Loans Board (HELB) in Kenya. (7 marks)

   b) Discuss four causes of Maji Maji uprising in Tanzania. (8 marks)

Question 9

   a) Identify five ethical considerations that an ECDE teacher trainee should ensure when making preparations for data collection for research. (5 marks)

   b) Explain five ways in which research data would be useful in ECDE. (10 marks)
