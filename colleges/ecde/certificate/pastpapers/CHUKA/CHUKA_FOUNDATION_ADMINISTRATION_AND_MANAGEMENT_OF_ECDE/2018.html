2018

Paper: ECDE 00111

Body: Chuka University

Instructions

Answer Question ONE and any other TWO Questions. Do not write on the Question paper.

Questions

Question 1

   a) State the importance of early childhood teacher training at the University. (5 Marks)

   b) Describe the following concepts: (6 Marks)

      i) Foundation

      ii) Administration

      iii) Management

   c) State five challenges facing Early Childhood Development and Education in Kenya. (5 Marks)

   d) State four roles played by a manager in an Early Childhood Center. (4 Marks)

   e) State five factors that may have contributed to the development of Early Childhood programmes during the colonial period. (5 Marks)

   f) State five factors that are considered when developing teaching and learning materials at the pre-school level of learning. (5 Marks)

Question 2

   a) Explain the roles of parents as care givers and stakeholders in an ECDE Programme implementation. (5 Marks)

   b) Discuss four steps that are observed in the establishment of an early childhood learning centre. (14 Marks)

Question 3

   a) Describe the legal requirements that must be fulfilled during the establishment of an early childhood learning centre in Kenya. (10 Marks)

   b) Suggest any five strategies that can be explored to motivate the teaching staff in a pre-school set up at the County levels in Kenya. (10 Marks)

Question 4

   a) Discuss the challenges faced by the public and private institutions offering pre-school teacher training in Kenya. (10 Marks)

   b) Explain the factors that may limit the expansion of early childhood services in both public and private schools. Suggest a solution for each factor. (10 Marks)
