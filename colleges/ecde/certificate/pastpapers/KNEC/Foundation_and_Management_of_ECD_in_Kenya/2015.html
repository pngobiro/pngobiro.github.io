2015

Certificate in Early Childhood Development and Education
Foundations and Management of ECDE - December 2015

Instructions to Candidates:

Write your name and index number in the spaces provided above. Sign and write the date of the examination in the spaces provided above. This question paper consists of TWO sections; A and B. Answer ALL the questions in section A and any FOUR questions from section B. Answers to both section A and B must be written in the spaces provided in this booklet. Do NOT remove any pages from this booklet. Candidates should answer the questions in English.

Question 1

(a) Define the term 'Laissez-Faire' leadership style.

Sample Answer:

Laissez-Faire is a leadership style where leaders provide minimal guidance and allow team members to make decisions independently.

Question 2

(b) Give four negative effects of a military form of government.

Sample Answer:

  1. Suppression of freedoms and human rights.
  2. Potential for corruption and abuse of power.
  3. Lack of transparency and accountability.
  4. Restriction of political participation.

Question 3

(a) Outline four uses of a log book in an ECDE center.

Sample Answer:

  • Tracking daily activities and attendance.
  • Recording important communications and messages.
  • Documenting incidents and accidents.
  • Monitoring inventory and resource usage.

Question 4

(b) Identify six functions of the Teacher’s Service Commission (TSC) in ECDE in Kenya.

Sample Answer:

  1. Recruitment and placement of teachers.
  2. Professional development and training.
  3. Ensuring teacher accountability and discipline.
  4. Managing teacher salaries and benefits.
  5. Providing support for curriculum implementation.
  6. Facilitating teacher welfare and representation.

Question 5

(a) (i) Define the term objective.

Sample Answer:

An objective is a specific, measurable, and achievable goal to be attained within a certain time frame.

Question 6

(b) Explain four ways a teacher would use to promote moral values among pre-school children.

Sample Answer:

  • Modeling ethical behavior.
  • Encouraging empathy and kindness.
  • Teaching the consequences of actions.
  • Incorporating moral stories and lessons.
