2019

Certificate in Early Childhood Development and Education
Foundations and Management of ECDE - April 2019

Instructions to Candidates:

Write your name and index number in the spaces provided above. Sign and write the date of the examination in the spaces provided above. This question paper consists of TWO sections; A and B. Answer ALL the questions in section A and any FOUR questions from section B. Answers to both section A and B must be written in the spaces provided in this booklet. Do NOT remove any pages from this booklet. Candidates should answer the questions in English.

Question 1

(a) Define the term communication.

Sample Answer:

Communication is the process of exchanging information, ideas, and feelings between individuals through various methods.

Question 2

(a) Explain the concept of Democracy.

Sample Answer:

Democracy is a form of government where power is held by the people, either directly or through elected representatives.

Question 3

(a) Explain the concept of legal provision.

Sample Answer:

Legal provision refers to the laws and regulations established to protect and ensure the rights and welfare of individuals within a society.

Question 4

(a) Define the term analysis as applied in Research.

Sample Answer:

Analysis in research involves the systematic examination and interpretation of data to uncover patterns, relationships, and insights.

Question 5

(a) Outline seven tips of public speaking required by a pre-school teacher during community capacity building.

Sample Answer:

  1. Clarity in speech.
  2. Engage the audience.
  3. Use appropriate body language.
  4. Be confident and poised.
  5. Speak at an appropriate pace.
  6. Use visual aids effectively.
  7. Practice beforehand.

Question 6

(a) Outline seven functions of the Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development in early childhood development and education.

Sample Answer:

  1. Curriculum development.
  2. Training of educators.
  3. Resource provision.
  4. Research and innovation.
  5. Quality assurance.
  6. Policy formulation.
  7. Public engagement.

Question 7

(a) Identify seven ways a teacher would use teaching materials in management of a pre-school class.

Sample Answer:

  1. Enhancing learning experiences.
  2. Supporting diverse learning styles.
  3. Encouraging creativity.
  4. Facilitating group activities.
  5. Assessing student progress.
  6. Providing visual aids.
  7. Stimulating curiosity and exploration.

Question 8

(a) A researcher intends to carry out a study on factors affecting transition of learners in an ECDE centre.

Sample Answer:

  1. Identify the research design suitable for the study.
  2. Consider ethical guidelines in research.
  3. Ensure clarity and specificity in objectives.
  4. Define the scope and limitations of the study.
  5. Establish a clear timeline for the research process.

(b) (i) Define the term leadership.

(ii) Identify seven ways a community leader would use Democratic Leadership pattern to manage ECDE projects.

Sample Answer:

(i) Leadership is the process of guiding, influencing, and motivating a group of people towards achieving a common goal.

(ii) Seven ways:

  1. Encouraging participation from all members.
  2. Holding regular meetings for discussions and decision-making.
  3. Valuing and considering input from community members.
  4. Ensuring transparency in decision-making processes.
  5. Facilitating consensus-building among stakeholders.
  6. Empowering community members to take ownership of projects.
  7. Promoting equality and fairness in the distribution of resources and responsibilities.
