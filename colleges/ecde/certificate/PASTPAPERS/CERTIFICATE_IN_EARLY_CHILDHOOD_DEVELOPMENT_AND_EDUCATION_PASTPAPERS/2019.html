CERTIFICATE IN EARLY CHILDHOOD DEVELOPMENT AND EDUCATION - 2019

Paper: 7012 Exam Body: THE KENYA NATIONAL EXAMINATIONS COUNCIL

Instructions

Write your name and index number in the spaces provided above. Sign and write the date of the examination in the spaces provided above. This question paper consists of TWO sections: A and B. Answer ALL the questions in section A and any FOUR questions from section B. Answers to both section A and B must be written in the spaces provided in this booklet. Do NOT remove any pages from this booklet. Candidates should answer the questions in English.

Questions

Question 1

   a) Define the term communication. (2 marks)

   b) Identify three factors that determine the choice of the mode of communication during community capacity building. (3 marks)

   c) Outline five barriers to effective communication during community capacity building. (5 marks)

Question 2

   a) Explain the concept of Democracy. (2 marks)

   b) Give the title of the persons in charge of Early Childhood Development and Education (ECDE) at the following education administrative offices in Kenya: (i) national level; (1 mark) (ii) regional level; (1 mark) (iii) county level; (1 mark) (iv) sub county. (1 mark)

   c) State four legal procedures that must be undertaken before conducting a general election in Kenya. (4 marks)

Question 3

   a) Explain the concept of legal provision. (2 marks)

   b) Identify four legal provisions for children that parents should undertake in Kenya. (4 marks)

   c) List four financial records that a pre-school teacher should maintain. (4 marks)

Question 4

   a) Define the term analysis as applied in Research. (2 marks)

   b) Identify three reasons for acknowledging source of data in Research. (3 marks)

   c) Outline five considerations a teacher trainee should make during identification of a research problem in ECDE. (5 marks)

Question 5

   a) Outline seven tips of public speaking required by a pre-school teacher during community capacity building. (7 marks)

   b) Explain four qualities of an effective community mobiliser. (8 marks)

Question 6

   a) Outline seven functions of the Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development in early childhood development and education. (7 marks)

   b) Discuss four effects of the Mau Mau uprising in Kenya. (8 marks)

Question 7

   a) Identify seven ways a teacher would use teaching materials in management of a pre-school class. (7 marks)

   b) State eight benefits of a syllabus to a pre-school teacher. (8 marks)

Question 8

   a) A researcher intends to carry out a study on factors affecting transition of learners in an ECDE centre. (i) Explain the appropriate type of Research design that will be used. (2 marks) (ii) Identify five guidelines to consider in formulating research objectives for the study. (5 marks)

   b) Explain four qualities of a good questionnaire. (8 marks)

Question 9

   a) Table 1 below shows the attendance of teachers trained on Curriculum Development. (i) The percentage of male teachers trained. (2 marks) (ii) The percentage of female teachers trained. (2 marks) (iii) The mean for female teachers trained. (2 marks)

Level Male Female
Middle class 2 5
Pre-unit 4 12
Standard one 13
Standard two 1 8
Standard three 2 19

   b) (i) Define the term leadership. (2 marks) (ii) Identify seven ways a community leader would use Democratic Leadership pattern to manage ECDE projects. (7 marks)
