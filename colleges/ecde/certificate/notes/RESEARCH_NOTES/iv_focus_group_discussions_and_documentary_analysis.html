(iv) Focus Group Discussions and Documentary Analysis

Unstructured interviews with small groups interacting with each other and a focus group leader. Utilizes group dynamics.

Advantages

  • Gathers extensive information
  • Clarifies issues

Disadvantages

  • Time-consuming
  • Difficult data analysis
  • Requires skilled leader
