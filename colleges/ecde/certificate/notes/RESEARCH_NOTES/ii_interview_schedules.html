(ii) Interview Schedules

A technique used to understand attitudes, references, behaviors, and underlying motivations. Can be conducted individually or in groups. An interview schedule is a list of questions in their order of presentation.

Advantages

  • Real and factual data
  • High response rate
  • Accurate data

Disadvantages

  • Expensive
  • Requires skilled interviewers
  • Small sample sizes
