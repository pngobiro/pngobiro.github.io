IMPORTANCE OF RESEARCH

The following are some of the reasons why research is important in a community, school, or nation:

  • Research enables acquiring and generating new knowledge, especially on emerging challenges and issues.
  • It is a basis for funding projects based on research findings.
  • Enhances curiosity and interest.
  • Provides solutions to daily emerging issues and challenges.
  • Used to demonstrate expertise and competency of scholars.
  • Helps in gathering information using the scientific method.

Importance of Research in Early Childhood

  • Improving teaching and learning.
  • Policy development.
  • Provides knowledge that helps in understanding children.
  • Enables us to predict children's behavior.
