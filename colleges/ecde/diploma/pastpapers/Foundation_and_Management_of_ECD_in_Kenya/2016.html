2016

Certificate in Early Childhood Development and Education
Foundations and Management of ECDE - December 2016

Instructions to Candidates:

Write your name and index number in the spaces provided above. Sign and write the date of the examination in the spaces provided above. This question paper consists of TWO sections; A and B. Answer ALL the questions in section A and any FOUR questions from section B. Answers to both section A and B must be written in the spaces provided in this booklet. Do NOT remove any pages from this booklet. Candidates should answer the questions in English.

Question 1

(a) Define the term 'Laissez-Faire' leadership style.

Sample Answer:

Laissez-Faire is a leadership style where leaders provide minimal guidance and allow team members to make decisions independently.

Question 2

(b) Give five reasons that make integrity an important value for a pre-school teacher in the society.

Sample Answer:

  1. Builds trust with children and parents.
  2. Ensures consistency in teaching practices.
  3. Models ethical behavior for children.
  4. Enhances professional reputation.
  5. Promotes a safe and reliable learning environment.

Question 3

(c) State three characteristics of a socialism type of Government.

Sample Answer:

  • Public ownership of major industries.
  • Redistribution of wealth to promote equality.
  • Government planning of the economy.

Question 4

(a) Give four reasons for maintaining permanent store records in an ECDE center.

Sample Answer:

  1. Track inventory levels and usage.
  2. Ensure accountability and transparency.
  3. Support budgeting and financial planning.
  4. Facilitate auditing and inspection processes.

Question 5

(b) Identify the type of information that a Sub-County Early Childhood Coordinator would find in each of the following records kept in an ECDE center: (i) log book; (ii) visitors book.

Sample Answer:

(i) Log book: Daily activities, attendance records, incident reports.

(ii) Visitors book: Names, dates, purposes of visit, signatures of visitors.

Question 6

(a) Discuss four roles of a pre-school teacher in ensuring that children’s right to medical care is attained through immunization in Kenya.

Sample Answer:

  • Ensuring children's immunization records are up-to-date.
  • Organizing and participating in immunization campaigns.
  • Providing health education to parents and guardians.
  • Monitoring and reporting any health issues to authorities.
