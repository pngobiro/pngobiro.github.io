PAST PAPER 2017

KENYA SCHOOL OF GOVERNMENT

PROFICIENCY EXAMINATION FOR CLERICAL OFFICERS

AUGUST 2017

PAPER II

PAPER CODE: 201

ORGANIZATION AND FUNCTIONS OF GOVERNMENT

DATE: WEDNESDAY, 23 RD AUGUST 2017

TIME: 2.00 P.M. TO 4.00 P.M. (2 HOURS)

INSTRUCTIONS TO CANDIDATES

  1. This paper consists of three sections: A, B, and C.
  2. Section A consists of twelve compulsory questions carrying 40 marks.
  3. Section B consists of three compulsory questions each carrying 20 marks.
  4. Answer all questions on the answer booklet provided.

SECTION A ( COMPULSORY)

1. Identify four superior courts in Kenya. (4 marks)

2. State four ways in which a clerical officer can prevent loss of stationery in the office. (4 marks)

3. List four members of a County Assembly Service Board. (4 marks)

4. From the list below identify members of the National Executive.

  1. President
  2. Principal Secretary
  3. Deputy President
  4. Cabinet Secretary (3 marks)

5. Identify Commissions that are responsible for the following functions:

  1. Drawing up electoral boundaries
  2. Reviewing salaries of all state officers
  3. Recruitment of Judicial officers (3 marks)

6. State the meaning of the term ‘Government’.  (2 marks)

7. State three conditions a person must fulfil for appointment as a member of a board of a city. (3 marks)

8. Name the national government administrative officers in charge of the following administrative regions:

  1. Location
  2. Ward
  3. Sub county
  4. Sub location (4 marks)

9. Differentiate between:

  1. State organ
  2. State office (2 marks)

10. State whether the following state corporations are regulatory or service-based.

  1. Kenya Bureau of Standards
  2. National Hospital Insurance Fund
  3. National Bank of Kenya
  4. Capital Market Authority (4 marks)

11. The Teacher Service Commission keeps a register of teachers. State four reasons for this. (4 marks)

12. Match the following state corporations with their respective mandates:

Brand Kenya Board Enhancing the welfare of Kenyans by Preventing misleading market conduct
Kenya Investment Authority Create and maintain a distinct country image with uniquely distinguished products, services and concepts
Competition Authority of Kenya

Organizing investment promotion activities both

(3 marks)

SECTION B ( COMPULSORY)

13. (a) Explain five ways in which devolution has enhanced the provision of Early Childhood Education. (10 marks)

(b) Outline five ways in which the National Government promotes sports in Kenya. (10 marks)

14. (a) Explain five ways in which the public service principle of “public participation in policy making” can be implemented in a Ministry. (10 marks)

(b) Outline five ways in which a Principal Secretary can ensure prudent utilization of financial resources in a state department. (10 marks)

15. (a) Contravention of Chapter Six of the Constitution may disqualify a person from being elected as a member of parliament. Explain six other factors that may lead to such disqualification. (12 marks)

(b) Explain four challenges faced by the Kenya Ferry Services in offering services. (8 marks)
