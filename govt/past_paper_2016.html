PAST PAPER 2016

KENYA SCHOOL OF GOVERNMENT

PROFICIENCY EXAMINATION FOR CLERICAL OFFICERS

AUGUST, 2016

PAPER CODE: 201

ORGANIZATION AND FUNCTIONS OF GOVERNMENT

DATE: WEDNESDAY, 17 AUGUST 2016

TIME: 2.00PM-4.00PM (2 HOURS)

INSTRUCTIONS TO CANDIDATES:

1. This paper consists of two sections, A and B.

2. Section A consists of twelve compulsory questions carrying a total of 40 marks.

3. Section B consists of three compulsory questions each carrying 20 marks. Answer all three questions.

4. Answer all questions on the answer booklet provided.

SECTION A: COMPULSORY

1. Identify two Independent Offices in the Constitution of Kenya (2 marks)

2. State three roles of the Secretary to the Cabinet. (3 marks)

3. Rank the following officers in a state department in order of seniority

  1. Senior Assistant Director
  2. Office Administrative Assistant
  3. Senior Clerical Officer
  4. Assistant Director  (4 marks)

4. Identify three subordinate courts in Kenya ( 3 marks)

5. The following are statements about County Governments. State whether they are True or False

  1. The County Executive Committee implements County legislation
  2. The County Governor can hold office for more than two terms
  3. The Speaker of the County Assembly can vote on a question/motion before the
    Assembly
  4. The County Governor is a member of the County Executive Committee (4 marks)

6. State two conditions necessary for one to be appointed in the office of Kadhi (2 marks)

7. State four factors that can lead to the removal of a member of the County Executive Committee from office (4marks)

8. From the list below identify three officers who serve in the National Government

  1. Cabinet Secretary
  2. Chief
  3. Ward Administrator
  4. Deputy County Commissioners
  5. Sub-County Administrator (3 marks)

9. State whether the following are Public officers or State officers

  1. Cabinet Secretary
  2. Director in a State department
  3. Clerical Officer
  4. High Court Judge (4 marks)

10. Which of the following are state corporations in the Ministry of Tourism

  1. Kenya Utalii College
  2. Kenya Safari Lodges and Hotels
  3. Kenya Wildlife Service
  4. National Environment Management
  5. Bomas of Kenya
  6. Kenya Water Towers Agency (3 marks)

11. State four factors that the County Public Service Board should consider when redesignating an officer (4 marks)

12. Identify the State Corporations responsible for the following functions

  1. Kenya Bureau of Statistics
  2. Communications Authority of Kenya
  3. Commission for University Education
  4. Kenya Boardcasting Corporation (4 marks)

SECTION B

13. a) Explain five benefits of acquisition of computer skills by a Clerical Officer (10 marks)

b) Explain five disciplinary measures that can be meted out against a Clerical officer for disclosure of confidential information. (10 marks)

14. (a) The County Government may elevate an urban area into a town. Highlight five requirements that an urban area should meet in order to be conferred the status of a town. (10 marks)

b) Highlight five functions of the Supreme Court of Kenya (10 marks)

15. (a) Explain five contributions of the Kenya National Highways Authority in the economic development of the country (10 marks)

b) Explain five ways in which the National Government ensures security of Kenyans (10 marks)
