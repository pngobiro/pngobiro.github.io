Centripetal Force

  • This is a force which acts on a body by directing the body towards its centre. Since the direction is continuously changing, the velocity therefore cannot be constant.
  • Applying Newton’s second law of motion(F=ma), centripetal force Fc is given by;
    Fc = ma = mv2/r. Since v = rω,
    Fc = mr2ω2/r = mω2r
  • The centripetal acceleration in relation to angular velocity ‘a’, is given by a=rω2,
